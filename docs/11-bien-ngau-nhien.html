Bin ngu nhin ri rc v lin tc

by Maxwell Margenot, revisions by Delaney Granizo Mackenzie

Edited by AI Cafe

Bin ngu nhin l bin c gi tr ph thuc vo xc sut. Khi tho lun v bin ngu nhin, chng ta thng m t chng theo cc phn phi xc sut. iu ny c ngha l xc sut m mi gi tr c th xut hin t bin ngu nhin. V d c in ca iu ny l xc xc, c th to ra cc gi tr t 1 n 6 vi xc sut ng u.

Chng ta thng phn loi bin ngu nhin thnh hai loi khc nhau:

  • Bin ngu nhin ri rc
  • Bin ngu nhin lin tc

Cch x l mi loi ny khc nhau, nhng cc nguyn tc c bn vn gi nguyn. Chng ta c th d dng thy cch m hnh ha bin ngu nhin c th hu ch khi x l ti chnh; ti sn ti chnh thng c biu din l chuyn ng theo cc mu xc nh v ngu nhin, vi cc mu ngu nhin c biu din bng bin ngu nhin. lm iu ny, chng ta s "ly mu" t bin ngu nhin ti mi bc thi gian, sau di chuyn cng c ti chnh theo mc . Phn tch ny c s dng v nhiu chuyn ng trong ti sn khng th gii thch bng cc m hnh xc nh.

Mi bin ngu nhin theo mt phn phi xc sut, l mt hm m t n. Phn phi xc sut gn xc sut cho tt c cc gi tr c th ca mt bin ngu nhin. i vi mt bin ngu nhin $X$, chng ta biu din xc sut rng $X$ bng mt gi tr $x$ l $P(X = x)$. i vi cc bin ngu nhin ri rc, chng ta c th biu din $p(x) = P(X = x)$ mt cch ngn gn. iu ny cng c bit n l hm khi xc sut (PMF). i vi cc bin ngu nhin lin tc, chng ta khng th s dng PMF, nh s cp sau, v vy chng ta phi s dng mt hm mt xc sut (PDF). Cc phn phi xc sut to thnh c s cho cc m hnh nh gi Black-Scholes v nh phn cng nh CAPM. S hiu bit v chng cng cn thit thc hin cc m phng Monte Carlo.

i vi mi hm phn phi xc sut, chng ta cng c mt hm phn phi tch ly (CDF). iu ny c nh ngha l $P(X \leq x)$, xc sut rng bin ngu nhin nh hn hoc bng mt gi tr c th. Cch vit tt cho CDF l $F(x) = P(X \leq x)$. tm $F(x)$ trong trng hp ri rc, chng ta cng tng cc gi tr ca PMF cho tt c cc kt qu nh hn hoc bng $x$. Trong trng hp lin tc, chng ta s dng php tnh tch phn PDF qua tt c cc gi tr n $x$.

Bin Ngu Nhin Ri Rc

Mt bin ngu nhin ri rc l mt bin c s kt qu c th m c. Mi kt qu ny c mt xc sut ring lin quan n n. Xem xt vic tung ng xu hoc ln xc xc, mt trong nhng bin ngu nhin phn phi u c bn nht. i vi vic tung ng xu, c hai kt qu c th xy ra, hoc l mt sp hoc mt nga, mi mt c xc sut l $1/2$. Cc bin ngu nhin ri rc khng phi lc no cng c trng s bng nhau cho tt c cc kt qu. n v c bn ca mt bin ngu nhin ri rc l hm khi xc sut (PMF), mt tn khc cho hm xc sut $p(x)$. PMF, hay hm xc sut, cung cp mt xc sut, mt khi lng, cho mi im trong min ca phn phi xc sut. Mt hm xc sut c hai thuc tnh chnh:

  1. $0 \leq p(x) \leq 1$ v tt c cc xc sut nm trong khong $[0, 1]$
  2. Tng ca tt c cc xc sut $p(x)$ trn tt c cc gi tr ca X phi bng $1$. Tng trng s cho tt c cc gi tr ca bin ngu nhin phi cng li thnh $1$.

y, chng ta s xem xt mt s v d v cc phn phi xc sut ri rc ph bin nht.

In[2]:
class DiscreteRandomVariable:
    def __init__(self, a=0, b=1):
        self.variableType = ""
        self.low = a
        self.high = b
        return
    def draw(self, numberOfSamples):
        samples = np.random.randint(self.low, self.high, numberOfSamples)
        return samples

Phn phi ng u

Loi phn phi xc sut c bn nht l phn phi ng u. Vi phn phi ng u ri rc, tt c cc kt qu u c gn trng s nh nhau. Ly v d v vic tung mt con xc xc. N c su mt, c nh s t $1$ n $6$, mi mt u c xc sut xy ra l $1/6$. Vi iu ny, chng ta bit rng hm phn phi xc sut (PMF) phi l $p(x) = 1/6$ cho tt c cc gi tr ca bin ngu nhin ng u $X$.

Vi vic tung xc sc, chng ta c th d dng thy rng $p(x) = 1/6$ cho tt c cc gi tr ca bin ngu nhin $X$. Hy xem xt cc kh nng cho tt c cc gi tr ca c hm xc sut v hm phn phi tch ly:

Value: $X = x$ | PMF: $p(x) = P(X = x)$ | CDF: $F(x) = P(X \leq x)$ | --- | --- | --- | 1 | $1/6$ | $1/6$ 2 | $1/6$ | $1/3$ 3 | $1/6$ | $1/2$ 4 | $1/6$ | $2/3$ 5 | $1/6$ | $5/6$ 6 | $1/6$ | $1$

S dng bng ny, chng ta c th d dng thy rng hm xc sut tha mn cc iu kin cn thit. Mi gi tr ca hm xc sut nm trong khong $[0,1]$, tha mn iu kin u tin. iu kin th hai cng c tha mn v tt c cc gi tr ca $p(x)$ cng li bng $1$, nh c chng minh trong hm phn phi tch ly. iu ny th hin hai tnh cht ca hm phn phi tch ly:

  1. CDF nm gia $0$ v $1$ cho mi gi tr ca $x$. iu ny tng ng vi gi tr ca hm phn phi xc sut.
  2. CDF khng gim theo $x$. iu ny c ngha l khi $x$ tng ln, CDF s tng ln hoc gi nguyn.

Khi c gng ly mu t cc phn phi xc sut khc, chng ta c th s dng s kt hp ca phn phi ng nht vi mt s hm nht nh c c cc mu ph hp. Tuy nhin, phng php ny c th rt km hiu qu. Do , chng ta s s dng cc hm c sn trong NumPy cho mi phn phi n gin ha vn .

Phn phi Nh thc

Phn phi nh thc c s dng m t cc thnh cng v tht bi. iu ny c th rt hu ch trong bi cnh u t v nhiu la chn ca chng ta c xu hng mang tnh nh phn nh vy. Khi chng ta thc hin mt th nghim thnh cng/tht bi n l, chng ta gi l th nghim Bernoulli. Vi bin ngu nhin Bernoulli, chng ta c hai kt qu c th xy ra:

$$p(1) = P(Y = 1) = p \ \ \ \ \ \ \ p(0) = P(Y = 0) = 1-p$$

Chng ta coi $Y$ nhn gi tr $1$ l mt thnh cng, v vy xc sut ca mt thnh cng xy ra trong mt th nghim n l l $p$.

Phn phi nh thc ly mt tp hp $n$ th nghim Bernoulli. Do , chng ta c th c t $0$ n $n$ thnh cng. Mi th nghim c xc sut thnh cng ging nhau, $p$, v tt c cc th nghim u c lp vi nhau. Chng ta c th m t ton b bin ngu nhin nh thc ch bng $n$ v $p$, c k hiu bng k hiu $X$ ~ $B(n, p)$. iu ny ni rng $X$ l mt bin ngu nhin nh thc vi cc tham s $n$ v $p$.

nh ngha hm xc sut ca mt bin ngu nhin nh thc, chng ta phi c kh nng chn mt s thnh cng no trong tng s th nghim. tng ny d dng lin quan n tng t hp trong t hp hc. Mt t hp m t tt c cc cch c th chn cc mc t mt tp hp sao cho th t khng quan trng. V d, nu chng ta c $6$ i tt v mun chn $2$ trong s , chng ta s vit tng s t hp c th l $\binom{6}{2}$. iu ny c m rng nh sau:

$$ \binom{6}{2} = \frac{6!}{4! \ 2!} = 15 $$

y, k hiu $!$ biu th giai tha v $n! = (n)(n-1)(n-2)\ldots (1)$. vit cng thc cho s kt hp mt cch tng qut hn, chng ta vit:

$$ \binom{n}{x} = \frac{n!}{(n-x)! \ x!} $$

Chng ta s dng k hiu ny chn cc thnh cng vi bin ngu nhin nh thc ca chng ta. S kt hp phc v mc ch tnh ton c bao nhiu cch khc nhau t c cng mt kt qu. Hm xc sut l:

$$ p(x) = P(X = x) = \binom{n}{x}p^x(1-p)^{n-x} = \frac{n!}{(n-x)! \ x!} p^x(1-p)^{n-x} $$

Vi $X$ l mt bin ngu nhin nh thc vi phn phi $B(n, p)$.

In[5]:
class BinomialRandomVariable(DiscreteRandomVariable):
    def __init__(self, numberOfTrials=10, probabilityOfSuccess=0.5):
        self.variableType = "Binomial"
        self.numberOfTrials = numberOfTrials
        self.probabilityOfSuccess = probabilityOfSuccess
        return
    def draw(self, numberOfSamples):
        samples = np.random.binomial(self.numberOfTrials, self.probabilityOfSuccess, numberOfSamples)
        return samples

Ly v d v gi c phiu di chuyn ln hoc xung, mi trng hp u c xc sut $p = 0.5$. Chng ta c th coi mt ln tng, hay $U$, l mt thnh cng v mt ln gim, hay $D$, l mt tht bi. Bin ngu nhin lin tc

Bin ngu nhin lin tc khc vi bin ngu nhin ri rc ch bin lin tc c th nhn v s kt qu. Chng khng th c m hoc m t di dng danh sch. Do , vic gn xc sut cho tng kt qu l v ngha. Bi v c v s kt qu, xc sut t c bt k kt qu c nhn no l 0.

Chng ta c th gii quyt vn ny bng cch thay vo tnh xc sut cho cc khong kt qu. iu ny c qun l thng qua vic s dng php tnh, mc d s dng cc k thut ly mu ca chng ta y, chng ta thc s khng cn phi s dng bt k iu no. i vi mt bin ngu nhin lin tc, $P(X = 0)$ l v ngha. Thay vo , chng ta s tm kim mt ci g ging nh $P(-1 < X < 1)$. i vi cc bin ngu nhin lin tc, thay v s dng hm PMF, chng ta nh ngha mt hm mt xc sut (PDF), $f_X(x)$, chng ta c th ni rng: $$P(a < X < b) = \int_a^b f_X(x)dx$$

Tng t nh yu cu ca chng ta i vi phn phi ri rc rng tt c xc sut cng li bng $1$, y chng ta yu cu rng:

  1. $f_X(x) \geq 0$ cho tt c cc gi tr ca $X$
  2. $P(-\infty < X < \infty) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f_X(x) dx = 1$

Cn lu rng v xc sut ti mt im c nhn vi phn phi lin tc l $0$, nn xc sut cc im bin ca mt khong cng l $0$. Do , $P(a \leq X \leq b) = P(a < X \leq b) = P(a \leq X < b) = P(a < X < b)$. Nu chng ta tch phn PDF qua tt c cc kh nng, trn ton b khong c th, gi tr nn l $1$.

class ContinuousRandomVariable:
    def __init__(self, a=0, b=1):
        self.variableType = ""
        self.low = a
        self.high = b
        return
    def draw(self, numberOfSamples):
        samples = np.random.uniform(self.low, self.high, numberOfSamples)
        return samples

Phn phi ng nht

Phn phi ng nht cng c th c nh ngha trong khun kh ca mt bin ngu nhin lin tc. Chng ta ly $a$ v $b$ l cc hng s, trong $b$ l gi tr cao nht c th v $a$ l gi tr thp nht c th m kt qu c th t c. Sau , hm mt xc sut (PDF) ca mt bin ngu nhin ng nht l:

$$f(x) = \begin{cases}\frac{1}{b - a} & \text{nu $a < x < b$} \\ 0 & \text{ngc li}\end{cases}$$

V hm ny c nh ngha trn mt khong lin tc, PDF bao ph tt c cc gi tr gia $a$ v $b$. Di y l mt th ca PDF (bn c th thay i gi tr ca $a$ v $b$ ty ):

In[10]:
a = 0.0
b = 8.0
x = np.linspace(a, b, 100)
y = [1/(b-a) for i in x]
plt.plot(x, y)
plt.xlabel('Value')
plt.ylabel('Probability');

Ging nh trong trng hp phn phi ng nht ri rc, hm mt xc sut (PDF) ca phn phi ng nht lin tc l khng i cho tt c cc gi tr m bin c th nhn. S khc bit duy nht y l chng ta khng th ly xc sut cho bt k im c nhn no. Hm phn phi tch ly (CDF), m chng ta c c t vic tch phn PDF, l:

$$ F(x) = \begin{cases} 0 & \text{i vi $x \leq a$} \\ \frac{x - a}{b - a} & \text{i vi $a < x < b$} \\ 1 & \text{i vi $x \geq b$}\end{cases}$$

V c v trn cng mt khong nh PDF nh sau:

In[11]:
y = [(i - a)/(b - a) for i in x]
plt.plot(x, y)
plt.xlabel('Value')
plt.ylabel('Probability');

Phn phi chun

Phn phi chun l mt phn phi rt ph bin v quan trng trong thng k. Nhiu bi kim nh v phng php quan trng trong thng k, v do , trong ti chnh, da trn gi nh v tnh chun. Mt phn ln iu ny l nh vo kt qu ca nh l gii hn trung tm (CLT), cho rng cc mu ln ca cc th nghim c lp s c phn phi theo phn phi chun. S tin li ca phn phi chun cng tm thy ng dng trong mt s chin lc giao dch thut ton. V d, nh cp trong ti liu bi ging giao dch cp (pair trading), chng ta c th tm kim cc cp c phiu c tnh ng lin kt v t cc vo hng thay i ca chnh lch gia chng da trn phn phi chun.

In[12]:
class NormalRandomVariable(ContinuousRandomVariable):
    def __init__(self, mean=0, variance=1):
        ContinuousRandomVariable.__init__(self)
        self.variableType = "Normal"
        self.mean = mean
        self.standardDeviation = np.sqrt(variance)
        return
    def draw(self, numberOfSamples):
        samples = np.random.normal(self.mean, self.standardDeviation, numberOfSamples)
        return samples

Khi m t mt bin ngu nhin phn phi chun, chng ta ch cn bit gi tr trung bnh ($\mu$) v phng sai ($\sigma^2$, trong $\sigma$ l lch chun). Chng ta k hiu mt bin ngu nhin $X$ l bin ngu nhin phn phi chun bng cch ni rng $X$ ~ $N(\mu, \sigma^2)$. Trong l thuyt danh mc u t hin i, li nhun ca c phiu thng c gi nh theo phn phi chun. Mt c im chnh ca bin ngu nhin phn phi chun l mt t hp tuyn tnh ca hai hoc nhiu bin ngu nhin phn phi chun s to thnh mt bin ngu nhin phn phi chun khc. iu ny rt hu ch khi xem xt li nhun trung bnh v phng sai ca mt danh mc u t gm nhiu c phiu. n thi im ny, chng ta ch xem xt phn phi xc sut mt bin, hay phn phi n bin. Khi chng ta mun m t nhiu bin ngu nhin cng mt lc, nh trong trng hp quan st nhiu c phiu, chng ta c th xem xt phn phi a bin. Mt phn phi chun a bin c m t hon ton bi cc gi tr trung bnh ca tng bin, phng sai ca chng, v cc tng quan ring bit gia tng cp bin. iu ny rt quan trng khi xc nh cc c im ca danh mc u t v phng sai ca danh mc u t tng th ph thuc vo c phng sai ca cc chng khon v cc tng quan gia chng.

Hm mt xc sut (PDF) ca mt bin ngu nhin phn phi chun l:

$$ f(x) = \frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}}e^{-\frac{(x - \mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}} $$

V c nh ngha cho $-\infty < x < \infty$. Khi chng ta c $\mu = 0$ v $\sigma = 1$, chng ta gi y l phn phi chun chun.

Bng cch thay i gi tr trung bnh v lch chun ca phn phi chun, chng ta c th thay i su v rng ca ng cong hnh chung. Vi lch chun ln hn, cc gi tr ca phn phi s t tp trung xung quanh gi tr trung bnh.

Thay v s dng phn phi chun m hnh ha gi c phiu, chng ta s dng n m hnh ha li sut. Gi c phiu khng th xung di $0$ trong khi phn phi chun c th nhn tt c cc gi tr trn ng thc, lm cho n ph hp hn vi li sut. Da trn gi tr trung bnh v phng sai ca mt phn phi chun, chng ta c th a ra cc tuyn b sau:

  • Khong $68\%$ tng s quan st nm trong mt lch chun xung quanh gi tr trung bnh ($\mu \pm \sigma$)
  • Khong $95\%$ tng s quan st nm trong hai lch chun xung quanh gi tr trung bnh ($\mu \pm 2\sigma$)
  • Khong $99\%$ tng s quan st nm trong ba lch chun xung quanh gi tr trung bnh ($\mu \pm 3\sigma$)

Nhng gi tr ny rt quan trng hiu cc khong tin cy lin quan n phn phi chun. Khi xem xt gi tr trung bnh v phng sai ca mt phn phi mu, chng ta thng xem xt cc khong tin cy khc nhau xung quanh gi tr trung bnh.

S dng nh l gii hn trung tm, chng ta c th chun ha cc bin ngu nhin khc nhau chng tr thnh cc bin ngu nhin chun. Mt cng c rt ph bin trong thng k l bng xc sut chun, c s dng tra cu cc gi tr ca CDF chun cho cc gi tr $x$ nht nh. Bng cch chuyn i cc bin ngu nhin thnh bin ngu nhin chun, chng ta c th d dng kim tra cc bng ny tm gi tr xc sut. Chng ta chun ha mt bin ngu nhin $X$ bng cch tr gi tr trung bnh v chia cho phng sai, dn n bin ngu nhin chun $Z$.

$$ Z = \frac{X - \mu}{\sigma} $$

Hy xem xt trng hp m $X$ ~ $B(n, p)$ l mt bin ngu nhin nh phn. Trong trng hp ca mt bin ngu nhin nh phn, gi tr trung bnh l $\mu = np$ v phng sai l $\sigma^2 = np(1 - p)$.
