S Dng M Hnh Yu T Xc nh Ri Ro

Chng ta c th s dng m hnh yu t phn tch cc ngun ri ro v li nhun trong danh mc u t. Nh rng mt m hnh yu t biu th li nhun nh sau:

$$R_i = a_i + b_{i1} F_1 + b_{i2} F_2 + \ldots + b_{iK} F_K + \epsilon_i$$

Bng cch m hnh ha cc li nhun lch s, chng ta c th thy bao nhiu trong s l do u c vo cc yu t khc nhau v bao nhiu l do s bin ng ring ca ti sn ($\epsilon_p$). Chng ta cng c th xem xt nhng ngun ri ro m danh mc u t ang phi i mt.

Trong phn tch ri ro, chng ta thng m hnh ha li nhun ch ng (li nhun so vi mt ch s tham chiu) v ri ro ch ng ( lch chun ca li nhun ch ng, cn c gi l sai s theo di hoc ri ro theo di).

V d, chng ta c th tm ng gp bin ca mt yu t i vi ri ro ch ng bnh phng (FMCAR). i vi yu t $j$, iu ny l:

$$ \text{FMCAR}_j = \frac{b_j^a \sum_{i=1}^K b_i^a Cov(F_j, F_i)}{(\text{Ri ro ch ng})^2} $$

Trong $b_i^a$ l mc tip xc ch ng ca danh mc u t vi yu t $i$. iu ny cho chng ta bit mc ri ro m chng ta phi chu khi tip xc vi yu t $j$, da trn tt c cc yu t khc m chng ta tip xc.

Cc m hnh yu t c bn thng c s dng nh gi cc danh mc u t v chng tng ng trc tip vi cc la chn u t (v d: liu chng ta c u t vo c phiu nh hay c phiu ln, v.v.). Di y, chng ta xy dng mt m hnh nh gi mt ti sn n l; bit thm thng tin v vic xy dng m hnh, hy tham kho bi ging m hnh yu t c bn.

Chng ta s s dng cc yu t Fama-French chun cho v d ny, l li nhun ca cc danh mc u t c xy dng da trn cc yu t c bn.

Bn mun bao nhiu yu t?

Trong bi ging v L thuyt nh gi chnh lch, chng ta cp rng i vi cc m hnh d on, bn mun c t tham s hn. Tuy nhin, iu ny khng hon ton ng i vi ri ro. Thay v c gng khng lm qu khp mt m hnh d on, bn ang tm kim bt k yu t ri ro no c th nh hng n li nhun ca bn. Do , thc t l an ton hn khi c lng mc tip xc vi nhiu yu t ri ro xem liu c bt k yu t no hiu qu. Bt k iu g cn li trong $\alpha$ ca chng ta l mc tip xc vi ri ro hin khng c gii thch bi cc yu t chn. Bn mun dng li nhun ca chin lc ca mnh hon ton l alpha, v khng c gii thch bi cng nhiu tham s cng tt. Nu bn c th ch ra rng li nhun lch s ca bn t hoc khng ph thuc vo nhiu yu t, iu ny rt tch cc. Chc chn rng mt s yu t ri ro khng lin quan c th c mi quan h gi to theo thi gian trong mt tp d liu ln, nhng nhng iu khng c kh nng nht qun.

tickers = np.array(universe('VN100')[:'2023'].iloc[-1].dropna().index)

start_date, end_date = '2024-01-01', '2024-12-31'

# Prices Data
prices = get_prices(*tickers, start_date='2023-12-01', end_date=end_date, field=['close', 'share', 'unadjusted'])
market_caps = 1000 * prices.xs('unadjusted', axis=1, level=1) * prices.xs('share', axis=1, level=1)
closes = prices.xs('close', axis=1, level=1).ffill()
returns = closes.pct_change(fill_method=None)[start_date:]

# Fundamental Data
book_value = fin_std({'_': 302, 'bs26857059': '309+308', 'bs7037699336': 4, 'bs1683709501': '302+303'})
BOOK = pd.DataFrame()
for ticker in tickers:
  bsq = get_fin_stmt(ticker, 'bsq', True)
  bsd = fin_stmt_date_idx(bsq)
  bv = book_value(bsd)
  bv = bv[~bv.index.duplicated(keep='last')]
  BOOK = BOOK.join(bv.to_frame(ticker), how='outer')

book_to_price = date_reindex(BOOK, market_caps.index) / market_caps

# Size
cap_rank = market_caps.rank(axis=1, ascending=False)
R_biggest = returns[cap_rank < 31].mean(axis=1)
R_smallest = returns[cap_rank > 70].mean(axis=1)

# Value
value_rank = book_to_price.rank(axis=1, ascending=False)
R_highpb = returns[value_rank < 31].mean(axis=1)
R_lowpb = returns[value_rank > 70].mean(axis=1)

# Benchmark
M = get_prices('VN100', frequency='index', end_date=end_date).VN100.pct_change()[start_date:]

# Defining our final factors
SMB = R_smallest - R_biggest # small minus big
HML = R_highpb - R_lowpb # high minus low

Danh mc yu t v theo di

Chng ta c th s dng danh mc yu t v danh mc theo di iu chnh nhy ca mt danh mc u t i vi cc ngun ri ro khc nhau.

Mt danh mc yu t c nhy l 1 i vi mt yu t c th v 0 i vi tt c cc yu t khc. Ni cch khc, n i din cho ri ro ca yu t . Chng ta c th thm mt danh mc yu t vo mt danh mc ln hn iu chnh mc tip xc ca n vi yu t .

Mt khi nim tng t l danh mc theo di, c xy dng c cng nhy yu t nh mt ch s tham chiu hoc danh mc khc. Ging nh danh mc yu t, iu ny cho php chng ta u c hoc phng nga ri ro lin quan n ch s tham chiu hoc danh mc . V d, chng ta thng xuyn phng nga ri ro th trng, v chng ta quan tm n vic danh mc u t ca mnh hot ng nh th no so vi th trng, v chng ta khng mun b nh hng bi s bin ng ca th trng.

xy dng mt danh mc yu t hoc danh mc theo di, chng ta cn nhy yu t ca nhng g chng ta mun theo di. Chng ta bit nhng iu ny trong trng hp trc, nhng chng ta cn tnh ton chng trong trng hp sau bng cc phng php m hnh yu t thng thng. Sau , chng ta chn mt s ti sn $K+1$ (ni $K$ l s lng yu t m chng ta ang xem xt) v tnh trng s ca cc ti sn trong danh mc.

nhy danh mc u t

nhy ca danh mc u t c th c tnh trc tip t dng li nhun, hoc nh l trung bnh trng s ca tt c cc ti sn ang nm gi.

V d

Gi s chng ta c hai yu t $F_1$ v $F_2$, v mt ch s tham chiu vi nhy ln lt l 1 v 1.1 i vi cc yu t ny. Chng ta xc nh 3 chng khon $x_1, x_2, x_3$ m chng ta mun s dng to thnh mt danh mc u t theo di ch s tham chiu, c nhy l $b_{11} = 0.7$, $b_{12} = 1.1$, $b_{21} = 0.1$, $b_{22} = 0.5$, $b_{31} = 1.5$, $b_{32} = 1.3$. Chng ta mun tnh ton cc trng s $w_1$, $w_2$, $w_3$ sao cho danh mc u t theo di ca chng ta l

$$ P = w_1 x_1 + w_2 x_2 + w_3 x_3 $$

Chng ta mun nhy ca danh mc u t khp vi ch s tham chiu:

$$ w_1 b_{11} + w_2 b_{21} + w_3 b_{31} = 1 $$ $$ w_1 b_{12} + w_2 b_{22} + w_3 b_{32} = 1.1 $$

Ngoi ra, cc trng s cn phi tng bng 1:

$$ w_1 + w_2 + w_3 = 1 $$

Gii h phng trnh tuyn tnh ny, chng ta tm c rng $w_1 = 1/3$, $w_2 = 1/6$, v $w_3 = 1/2$. Ghp cc chng khon li thnh mt danh mc u t s dng cc trng s ny, chng ta c c mt danh mc u t c cng h s ri ro vi ch s tham chiu.
