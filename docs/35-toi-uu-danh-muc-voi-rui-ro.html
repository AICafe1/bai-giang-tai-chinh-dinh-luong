Ti u ha danh mc u t c gii hn ri ro

Qun l ri ro l rt quan trng trong vic xy dng cc danh mc u t v pht trin cc thut ton. Chc nng chnh ca n l ci thin cht lng v tnh nht qun ca li nhun bng cch tnh ton y cc ri ro. Bt k li nhun no thu c t cc ri ro khng mong i, lun n cha trong danh mc u t ca chng ta, thng khng th c tin cy to ra li nhun trong mt thi gian di. Bng cch hn ch tc ng ca hoc loi b nhng ri ro khng mong i ny, danh mc u t l tng nn ch c s tip xc vi alpha m chng ta ang theo ui. Trong bi ging ny, chng ta s tp trung vo cch s dng cc m hnh yu t trong qun l ri ro.

M Hnh Yu T

Cc bi ging khc tho lun v M Hnh Yu T v cch tnh Ton b Ri ro Yu T, cng nh cch phn tch cc yu t alpha. K hiu m chng ta thng s dng khi gii thiu mt m hnh yu t nh sau:

$$R_i = a_i + b_{i1} F_1 + b_{i2} F_2 + \ldots + b_{iK} F_k + \epsilon_i$$

trong : $$\begin{eqnarray} k &=& \text{s lng yu t}\\ R_i &=& \text{li tc ca cng ty $i$}, \\ a_i &=& \text{im chn},\\ F_j &=& \text{li tc ca yu t $j$, $j \in [1,k]$}, \\ b_{ij} &=& \text{mc tip xc tng ng vi yu t $j$, $j \in [1,k]$,} \\ \epsilon_i &=& \text{s bin ng c th ca cng ty $i$.}\\ \end{eqnarray}$$

nh lng nhng ri ro khng lng trc v c c mc ri ro chp nhn trong mt danh mc u t nht nh, chng ta cn tr li 3 cu hi sau:

  1. T l ca phng sai trong danh mc u t ca ti n t cc yu t ri ro chung l bao nhiu?
  2. Ti lm th no gii hn ri ro ny?
  3. Li nhun/PNL ca danh mc u t ca ti n t u, tc l ti quy cho hiu sut ny vo ci g?

Cc yu t ri ro c th l:

  • Cc yu t c bn c in, chng hn nh nhng yu t trong CAPM (ri ro th trng) hoc M hnh Fama-French 3 yu t (t l gi trn s sch (P/B), bin ng)
  • S tip xc vi ngnh hoc lnh vc
  • Cc yu t v m, chng hn nh lm pht hoc li sut
  • Cc yu t thng k da trn li nhun lch s v c rt ra t phn tch thnh phn chnh

assets = np.array(universe('VN100')[:'2023'].iloc[-1].dropna().index)

start_date, end_date = '2023-01-01', '2024-12-31'

# Prices Data
prices = get_prices(*assets, start_date='2022-12-01', end_date=end_date, field=['close', 'share', 'unadjusted'])
market_caps = 1000 * prices.xs('unadjusted', axis=1, level=1) * prices.xs('share', axis=1, level=1)
closes = prices.xs('close', axis=1, level=1).ffill()
R = closes.pct_change(fill_method=None)[start_date:]

# Fundamental Data
book_value = fin_std({'_': 302, 'bs26857059': '309+308', 'bs7037699336': 4, 'bs1683709501': '302+303'})
BOOK = pd.DataFrame()
for ticker in assets:
  bsq = get_fin_stmt(ticker, 'bsq', True)
  bsd = fin_stmt_date_idx(bsq)
  bv = book_value(bsd)
  bv = bv[~bv.index.duplicated(keep='last')]
  BOOK = BOOK.join(bv.to_frame(ticker), how='outer')

book_to_price = date_reindex(BOOK, market_caps.index) / market_caps

# Size
cap_rank = market_caps.rank(axis=1, ascending=False)
R_biggest = R[cap_rank < 31].mean(axis=1)
R_smallest = R[cap_rank > 70].mean(axis=1)

# Value
value_rank = book_to_price.rank(axis=1, ascending=False)
R_highpb = R[value_rank < 31].mean(axis=1)
R_lowpb = R[value_rank > 70].mean(axis=1)

# Benchmark
M = get_prices('VN100', frequency='index', end_date=end_date).VN100.pct_change()[start_date:]

# Defining our final factors
SMB = R_smallest - R_biggest # small minus big
HML = R_highpb - R_lowpb # high minus low

Chy mt hi quy tuyn tnh a bin trn cc yu t c bn cho mi ti sn trong danh mc u t, chng ta c th thu c cc yu t ri ro tng ng cho mi ti sn. Ti y, chng ta din t:

$$ R_i = \alpha_i + \beta_{i, MKT} R_{i, MKT} + \beta_{i, HML} R_{i, HML} + \beta_{i, SMB} R_{i, SMB} + \epsilon_i$$

cho mi ti sn $S_i$. iu ny cho chng ta thy t l phn trm ca li nhun tng chng khon c to ra t cc yu t ri ro ny.

Phn Tch Phng Sai Thnh Cc Yu T Ri Ro Chung

Phng sai ca danh mc u t c th c biu din nh sau:

$$\sigma^2 = \omega BVB^{\top}\omega^{\top} + \omega D\omega^{\top}$$

Trong :

$$\begin{eqnarray} B &=& \text{ma trn th hin mc tip xc ca $n$ ti sn vi cc yu t} \\ V &=& \text{ma trn hip phng sai ca cc yu t} \\ D &=& \text{phng sai c th} \\ \omega &=& \text{vector trng s danh mc cho $n$ ti sn} \\ \omega BVB^{\top}\omega^{\top} &=& \text{phng sai yu t chung} \\ \omega D\omega^{\top} &=& \text{phng sai c th} \\ \end{eqnarray}$$

Tnh Ton Yu T Chung v Phng Sai c Th:

Chng ta s xy dng cc hm phn tch ri ro trong danh mc u t ca mnh. Gi s rng danh mc u t ca chng ta bao gm tt c cc c phiu trong VN100, vi trng s bng nhau. Hy cng xem mt cch c th rng bao nhiu phn trm phng sai ca li sut trong tp v tr ny l do yu t ri ro chung.

def compute_common_factor_variance(factors, factor_exposures, w):
    B = np.asarray(factor_exposures)
    F = np.asarray(factors)
    V = np.asarray(factors.cov())


    return w.dot(B.dot(V).dot(B.T)).dot(w.T)

common_factor_variance = compute_common_factor_variance(F, B, w)[0][0]
print("Common Factor Variance: {0}".format(common_factor_variance))

In[14]:
def compute_specific_variance(epsilon, w):

    D = np.diag(np.asarray(epsilon.var())) * epsilon.shape[0] / (epsilon.shape[0]-1)

    return w.dot(D).dot(w.T)

specific_variance = compute_specific_variance(epsilon, w)[0][0]
print("Specific Variance: {0}".format(specific_variance))

Specific Variance: 2.22583689919702e-06

tnh bao nhiu phn trm ca phng sai danh mc u t c cu thnh t yu t ri ro chung, chng ta thc hin nh sau:

$$\frac{\text{phng sai yu t chung}}{\text{phng sai yu t chung + phng sai c th}}$$

Ti u Ha C Gii Hn Ri Ro

Hin ti, chng ta ang vn hnh vi mt danh mc u t c trng s bng nhau. Tuy nhin, chng ta c th phn b li nhng trng s theo cch ti thiu ha ri ro yu t chung c minh ha bi cc mc tip xc vi yu t chung ca chng ta. y l mt bi ton ti u ha danh mc u t tm ra trng s ti u.

Chng ta nh ngha bi ton ny nh sau:

\begin{array}{ll} \mbox{$\text{ti thiu/ti a}$}_{w} & \text{hm mc tiu}\\ \mbox{vi iu kin} & {\bf 1}^T \omega = 1, \quad f=B^T\omega\\ & \omega \in {\cal W}, \quad f \in {\cal F}, \end{array}

Trong , bin $w$ l vector phn b, bin $f$ l cc mc tip xc vi yu t c trng s, v bin ${\cal F}$ cung cp cc rng buc cho $f$. Chng ta t ${\cal F}$ l mt vector gii hn cc mc tip xc vi yu t c trng s ca danh mc u t. Nhng rng buc ny cho php chng ta loi b cc trng s khng ph hp vi tiu ch ca chng ta. V d, chng ta c th t mc tip xc ti a m danh mc u t ca chng ta c th c bng cch thay i gi tr ca ${\cal F}$. Mt gi tr l $[1,1,1]$ s ch ra rng chng ta mun mc tip xc ti a ca danh mc u t vi mi yu t phi nh hn $1$, t chi bt k danh mc no khng p ng iu kin .

Chng ta nh ngha hm mc tiu l bt k mc tiu kinh doanh no m chng ta nh gi cao nht. y c th l mt ci g nh ti a ha t l Sharpe hoc ti thiu ha bin ng. Cui cng, iu chng ta mun gii quyt trong bi ton ti u ha ny l cc trng s, $\omega$.

Hy nhanh chng to mt s trng s ngu nhin xem cch thay i cc mc tip xc vi yu t c trng s ca danh mc u t.
