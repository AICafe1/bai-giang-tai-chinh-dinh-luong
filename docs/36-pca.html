Phn Tch Thnh Phn Chnh

by Rene Zhang and Max Margenot

Edited by AI Cafe

c ng dng trong nhiu lnh vc, chng hn nh x l hnh nh, sinh hc thng tin v ti chnh nh lng, lin quan n d liu quy m ln. C kch thc v phc tp ca d liu ny c th khin cc php ton cn thit cho vic phn tch tr nn kh kh thi. Phn Tch Thnh Phn Chnh (PCA) l mt phng php c in cho vic gim chiu. N s dng mt s thnh phn chnh u tin, cc c trng thng k gii thch phn ln bin ng ca ma trn d liu $m \times n$ $\mathbf{X}$, m t ma trn d liu quy m ln $\mathbf{X}$ mt cch hiu qu.

Chng ta s gii thiu PCA vi mt v d v x l hnh nh. Mt hnh nh k thut s mu xm c th c biu din bng mt ma trn, trong phn t $(i,j)^{th}$ tng ng vi gi tr mu xm ti pixel $(i,j)^{th}$. Hnh nh mu xm sau y c kch thc $200 \times 200$ pixel, mc d c th thay i linh hot. Chng ta lu n trong mt ma trn $\mathbf{X}$. S hng ca ma trn $\mathbf{X}$ l $200$, v s ct ca ma trn $\mathbf{X}$ cng l $200$.

def generate_test_image(m,n):
    X = np.zeros((m,n))
# generate a rectangle
    X[25:80,25:80] = 1
# generate a triangle
    for i in range(25, 80, 1):
        X[i+80:160, 100+i-1] = 2
# generate a circle
    for i in range(0,200,1):
        for j in range(0,200,1):
            if ((i - 135)*(i - 135) +(j - 53)*(j - 53) <= 900):
                X[i, j] = 3
    return X
X = generate_test_image(200,200)

t mi hng ca ma trn $\mathbf{X}$ thnh mt bin, vi cc quan st cc ct. Gi ma trn hip phng sai ca $\mathbf{X}$ l $\mathbf{C}$, vi kch thc ca $\mathbf{C}$ l $m \times m$. Ma trn $\mathbf{C}$ l mt ma trn m phn t v tr $(i,j)^{th}$ l hip phng sai gia hng th $i$ v hng th $j$ ca ma trn $\mathbf{X}$.

Phn tch thnh phn chnh (PCA) phn r ma trn $\mathbf{C}$ thnh:

$$\mathbf{C} = \mathbf{L}\mathbf{P}\mathbf{L}^{\top},$$

trong $\mathbf{P}$ l mt ma trn ng cho $\mathbf{P}=\text{diag}(\lambda_1,\lambda_2,\dots,\lambda_m)$, vi $\lambda_1 \geq \lambda_2 \geq \dots \geq \lambda_m \geq 0$ l cc gi tr ring ca ma trn $\mathbf{C}$. Ma trn $\mathbf{L}$ l mt ma trn trc giao, bao gm cc vect ring ca ma trn $\mathbf{C}$.

In[7]:
P, L = LA.eigh(C)

Hm LA.eigh lit k cc gi tr ring t nh n ln trong $P$. Hy thay i th t trc lit k chng t ln n nh v m bo rng $\mathbf{L}\mathbf{P}\mathbf{L}^{\top}==\mathbf{C}$.

L = L[:,::-1]

Thnh phn chnh th $i^{th}$ c cho bi hng th $i^{th}$ ca ma trn $\mathbf{V}$,

$$\mathbf{V} = \mathbf{L}^{\top} \mathbf{X}.$$

In[11]:
V = L.T.dot(X)
V.shape
Out[11]:
(200, 200)

Nu chng ta nhn c hai bn bn tri bi $\mathbf{L}$, chng ta s c:

$$\mathbf{L}\mathbf{L}^{\top} \mathbf{X}= \mathbf{L}\mathbf{V}.$$

Ma trn $\mathbf{L}$ l tp hp cc vect ring t mt ma trn hip phng sai, v vy $\mathbf{L}\mathbf{L}^{\top} = \mathbf{I}$ v $\mathbf{L}\mathbf{L}^{\top}\mathbf{X} = \mathbf{X}$. Mi quan h gia cc ma trn ca $\mathbf{X}$, $\mathbf{L}$, v $\mathbf{V}$ c th c din t nh sau:

$$\mathbf{X} = \mathbf{L}\mathbf{V}.$$

xp x $\mathbf{X}$, chng ta s dng $k$ vect ring c gi tr ring ln nht:

$$\mathbf{X} \approx \mathbf{L[:, 1:k]}\mathbf{L[:, 1:k]}^{\top} \mathbf{X}.$$

Gi $\tilde{\mathbf{X}}$ l xp x ca $\mathbf{X}$ nh sau: $\tilde{\mathbf{X}} = \mathbf{L[:, 1:k]}\mathbf{L[:, 1:k]}^{\top} \mathbf{X}$. Khi $k = m$, $\tilde{\mathbf{X}}$ s ging nh $\mathbf{X}$.

T l ca tng phng sai do thnh phn chnh th $i^{th}$ c cho bi t l $\frac{\lambda_i}{\lambda_1 + \lambda_2 + \dots + \lambda_m}$. Tng t l ca tng phng sai nn bng $1$. Nh chng ta nh ngha, $\lambda_i$ l mc th $i^{th}$ ca ma trn $\mathbf{P}$,

$$\sum_{i}\frac{P_i}{\text{trace}(P)} = 1$$

Trong trace$(P)$ l tng ca cc phn t cho ca ma trn $P$.

from sklearn.decomposition import PCA
num_pc = 2

X = np.asarray(portfolio_returns)
[n,m] = X.shape
print('The number of timestamps is {}.'.format(n))
print('The number of stocks is {}.'.format(m))

pca = PCA(n_components=num_pc) # number of principal components
pca.fit(X)

percentage =  pca.explained_variance_ratio_
percentage_cum = np.cumsum(percentage)
print('{0:.2f}% of the variance is explained by the first 2 PCs'.format(percentage_cum[-1]*100))

pca_components = pca.components_
