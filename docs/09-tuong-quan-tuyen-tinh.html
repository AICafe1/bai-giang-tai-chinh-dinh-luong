H s tng quan o lng mc m mi quan h gia hai bin l tuyn tnh. Gi tr ca n lun nm trong khong t -1 n 1. Mt h s dng cho thy cc bin c mi quan h trc tip, tc l khi mt bin tng, bin kia cng tng theo. Mt h s m cho thy cc bin c mi quan h ngc chiu, ngha l khi mt bin tng, bin kia s gim. Cng gn 0, h s tng quan cng cho thy mi quan h gia cc bin yu hn.

H s tng quan ca hai chui $X$ v $Y$ c nh ngha l $$r = \frac{Cov(X,Y)}{std(X)std(Y)}$$ trong $Cov$ l hip phng sai v $std$ l lch chun.

Hai tp d liu ngu nhin s c h s tng quan gn 0.

Tng quan vs. hip phng sai

Tng quan ch l mt hnh thc chun ha ca hip phng sai. V bn cht, chng ging nhau v thng c s dng mt cch tng i thay th cho nhau trong cc cuc tr chuyn hng ngy. R rng l vic s dng ngn ng chnh xc khi tho lun v hai khi nim ny l rt quan trng, nhng v mt khi nim, chng gn nh tng ng.

Hip phng sai khng c nhiu ngha khi ng mt mnh

Gi s chng ta c hai bin $X$ v $Y$ v chng ta ly hip phng sai ca hai bin ny.

iu ny c ngha l g? H s tng quan s dng thng tin v phng sai ca X v Y chun ha ch s ny. Khi chng ta chun ha ch s vo thang -1 n 1, chng ta c th a ra nhng nhn nh c ngha v so snh cc h s tng quan.

thy iu ny c thc hin nh th no, hy xem cng thc.

$$\frac{Cov(X, Y)}{std(X)std(Y)}$$

$$= \frac{Cov(X, Y)}{\sqrt{var(X)}\sqrt{var(Y)}}$$

$$= \frac{Cov(X, Y)}{\sqrt{Cov(X, X)}\sqrt{Cov(Y, Y)}}$$

minh ha iu ny, hy so snh h s tng quan v hip phng sai ca hai chui.

Ti sao c np.cov v np.corrcoef u tr v ma trn?

Ma trn hip phng sai l mt khi nim quan trng trong thng k. Thng thng, mi ngi s ni n hip phng sai ca hai bin $X$ v $Y$, nhng thc t ch l mt phn t trong ma trn hip phng sai ca $X$ v $Y$. i vi mi bin u vo, chng ta c mt hng v mt ct. ng cho chnh ch l phng sai ca bin , hay $Cov(X, X)$, cc phn t ngoi ng cho l cc hip phng sai gia cc bin khc nhau. Ma trn ny i xng qua ng cho. Hy kim tra xem iu ny c ng khng.

Mi Quan H Phi Tuyn

H s tng quan c th hu ch trong vic kim tra mnh ca mi quan h gia hai bin. Tuy nhin, iu quan trng l phi nh rng hai bin c th lin kt theo nhng cch khc nhau v c th d on c m phn tch ny khng th pht hin. V d, mt bin c th hon ton theo di hnh vi ca mt bin khc, nhng vi mt tr. C nhng k thut x l s tng quan tr ny. Ngoi ra, mt bin c th lin quan n t l thay i ca mt bin khc. Khng c mi quan h no trong s ny l tuyn tnh, nhng c th rt hu ch nu c pht hin.

Ngoi ra, h s tng quan c th rt nhy cm vi cc im ngoi l. iu ny c ngha l vic bao gm hoc loi tr ngay c mt vi im d liu cng c th thay i kt qu ca bn, v khng phi lc no cng r rng liu nhng im ny cha thng tin hay ch l nhiu.

V d, hy lm cho phn phi nhiu tr thnh phn phi Poisson thay v phn phi chun v xem iu g s xy ra.
