Tham S

Mt tham s l gi tr m mt m hnh s dng hn ch cc d on ca n. Thng thng, mt tham s l mt i lng gip m t mt tp d liu hoc mt phn phi. V d, trung bnh ca mt phn phi chun l mt tham s; thc t, chng ta ni rng mt phn phi chun c tham s ha bi trung bnh v phng sai ca n. Nu chng ta ly trung bnh ca mt tp hp cc mu c rt ra t phn phi chun, chng ta s c c mt c lng ca trung bnh ca phn phi. Tng t nh vy, trung bnh ca mt tp hp cc quan st l mt c lng ca tham s ca phn phi c s (m thng c gi nh l chun). Cc tham s khc bao gm trung v, h s tng quan vi mt chui khc, lch chun, v mi php o khc ca mt tp d liu.

Bn Khng Bao Gi Bit, Bn Ch c Lng

Khi tnh trung bnh ca mt tp d liu, bn khng bit chnh xc gi tr trung bnh. Bn c lng gi tr trung bnh tt nht c th t d liu bn c. S c lng ny c th khng chnh xc. iu ny ng vi bt k tham s no m bn c lng. thc s hiu iu g ang xy ra, bn cn xc nh chnh xc ca c lng ca mnh bng cch xem xt tnh n nh/ lch chun/ khong tin cy ca n.

S khng n nh ca cc c lng

Mi khi chng ta xem xt mt tp hp cc quan st, vic tnh ton mt tham s ch c th l mt c lng. N s thay i khi chng ta thc hin nhiu php o hn hoc khi thi gian tri qua v chng ta c c nhng quan st mi. Chng ta c th nh lng s khng chc chn trong c lng ca mnh bng cch xem xt cch m tham s thay i khi chng ta xem xt cc tp con khc nhau ca d liu. V d, lch chun m t mc khc nhau gia trung bnh ca mt tp hp v trung bnh ca tng quan st, ngha l, so vi tng quan st mt. Trong cc ng dng ti chnh, d liu thng xut hin di dng chui thi gian. Trong trng hp ny, chng ta c th c lng mt tham s ti cc thi im khc nhau; chng hn, trong 30 ngy trc . Bng cch xem xt mc dao ng ca c lng di ng ny khi chng ta thay i ca s thi gian ca mnh, chng ta c th tnh ton s khng n nh ca tham s c lng.

#Generate some data from a bi-modal distribution
def bimodal(n):
    X = np.zeros((n))
    for i in range(n):
        if np.random.binomial(1, 0.5) == 0:
            X[i] = np.random.normal(-5, 1)
        else:
            X[i] =  np.random.normal(5, 1)
    return X

X = bimodal(1000)

#Let's see how it looks

plt.xlabel('Value')
print('mean:', np.mean(X))
print('standard deviation:', np.std(X))

V d: T l Sharpe

Mt thng k thng c s dng m t hiu sut ca cc ti sn v danh mc u t l t l Sharpe, o lng li nhun b sung trn mi n v ri ro b sung m mt danh mc u t t c, so vi mt ngun li nhun khng c ri ro nh tri phiu kho bc hoc tin mt: $$R = \frac{E[r_a - r_b]}{\sqrt{Var(r_a - r_b)}}$$

Trong $r_a$ l li nhun trn ti sn ca chng ta v $r_b$ l t l li nhun khng c ri ro. Ging nh vi trung bnh v lch chun, chng ta c th tnh ton t l Sharpe cun xem cch c lng ca chng ta thay i theo thi gian.

from data import get_prices

def sharpe_ratio(asset, riskfree=0):
    return np.mean(asset - riskfree)/np.std(asset - riskfree)

start = '2022-01-01'
end = '2025-01-01'

closes = get_prices('FPT', start_date=start, end_date=end).FPT
returns = closes.pct_change()[1:] # Get the returns on the asset

running_sharpe = returns.rolling(90).apply(sharpe_ratio)

# Plot running Sharpe ratio up to 100 days before the end of the data set
_, ax1 = plt.subplots()
ax1.plot(running_sharpe[90:-100]);
plt.xlabel('Date')
plt.ylabel('Sharpe Ratio');

T l Sharpe c v kh bin ng, v r rng l ch th hin n di dng mt gi tr duy nht s khng hu ch nhiu cho vic d on cc gi tr trong tng lai. Thay vo , chng ta c th tnh ton trung bnh v lch chun ca d liu trn, v sau xem liu iu ny c gip chng ta d on t l Sharpe cho 100 ngy ti hay khng.

Kt lun

Mi khi chng ta tnh ton mt tham s cho mt b d liu, chng ta cng nn tnh ton bin ng ca n. Nu khng, chng ta khng bit liu cc im d liu mi c ph hp vi tham s ny hay khng. Mt cch tt tnh ton bin ng l chia d liu thnh cc tp con v c lng tham s t tng tp, sau tm bin thin gia cc kt qu. Vn c th c nhng yu t bn ngoi c a vo sau thi gian mu ca chng ta v m chng ta khng th d on. Tuy nhin, phn tch s khng n nh v kim tra sai s chun vn rt hu ch cho chng ta bit mc khng tin cy ca cc c lng ca mnh.

Lu : Ti liu ny mang tnh cht chia s kin thc khoa hc d liu. Cc m chng khon xut hin trong ti liu ch mang tnh cht minh ho, khng khuyn ngh u t. Chng ti khng chu trch nhim trc mi ri ro no do s dng ti liu ny.
