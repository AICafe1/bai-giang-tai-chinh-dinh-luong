Hi quy tuyn tnh a bin tng qut ha hi quy tuyn tnh, cho php bin ph thuc l mt hm tuyn tnh ca nhiu bin c lp. Nh trc y, chng ta gi nh rng bin $Y$ l mt hm tuyn tnh ca $X_1,\ldots, X_k$:

$$ Y_i = \beta_0 + \beta_1 X_{1i} + \ldots + \beta_k X_{ki} + \epsilon_i $$

Thng th trong ti chnh, dng ny s c vit nh sau, nhng ch c tn bin thay i v m hnh th vn ging nhau:

$$ Y_i = \alpha + \beta_1 X_{1i} + \ldots + \beta_k X_{ki} + \epsilon_i $$

i vi cc quan st $i = 1,2,\ldots, n$. tm mt phng (hoc siu phng) ph hp tt nht, chng ta s s dng phng php bnh phng ti thiu thng thng (OLS), phng php ny tm cch gim thiu sai s bnh phng gia cc d on v quan st, $\sum_{i=1}^n \epsilon_i^2$. Vic bnh phng lm cho sai s dng v m u xu nh nhau, v phng i cc sai s ln. N cng t ln v p ca ton hc n di m hnh tuyn tnh, nhng chng ta s khng i su vo iu by gi. c mt v d v sai s bnh phng, hy xem phn sau.

Gi s Y l d liu thc t ca chng ta, v Y_hat l nhng d on c thc hin bi hi quy tuyn tnh.

Mt khi chng ta s dng phng php ny xc nh cc h s ca hi quy, chng ta s c kh nng s dng cc gi tr quan st mi ca $X$ d on cc gi tr ca $Y$.

Mi h s $\beta_j$ cho chng ta bit $Y_i$ s thay i bao nhiu nu chng ta thay i $X_j$ mt n v trong khi gi tt c cc bin ph thuc khc khng i. iu ny cho php chng ta phn tch cc ng gp ca cc hiu ng khc nhau. iu ny gi nh rng m hnh tuyn tnh l m hnh chnh xc.

Chng ta bt u bng cch xy dng $Y$, $X_1$, v $X_2$ mt cch nhn to trong chng ta bit mi quan h chnh xc gia chng.

Cn phi ch n nhng kt qu ny ging nh vi cc o hm ring. Cng thc cho $Y$ c v nh l

$$X_1 + X_2 = X_1 + X^2 + X_1 = 2 X_1 + X^2$$

Hoc $2X_1$ cng vi mt parabol.

Tuy nhin, h s ca $X_1$ l 1. iu ny c ngha l $Y$ thay i 1 nu chng ta thay i $X_1$ thm 1 trong khi gi $X_2$ khng i. Hi quy tuyn tnh nhiu bin tch bit cc ng gp t cc bin khc nhau.

Tng t, vic thc hin hi quy tuyn tnh trn hai chng khon c th cho ra mt $\beta$ cao. Tuy nhin, nu chng ta a vo mt chng khon th ba (v d: VNINDEX) nh mt bin c lp, chng ta c th thy rng mi tng quan gia hai chng khon u tin hu nh hon ton do c hai u c tng quan vi VNINDEX. iu ny hu ch v VNINDEX c th l mt d on ng tin cy hn cho c hai chng khon so vi vic chng d on ln nhau. Phng php ny cho php chng ta nh gi tt hn mi lin quan gia hai chng khon v ngn chn s gy nhiu gia hai bin.

Gi nh ca M hnh

Tnh hp l ca cc thng k ny ph thuc vo vic cc gi nh ca m hnh hi quy tuyn tnh c c tha mn hay khng. Cc gi nh ny bao gm:

  • Bin c lp khng phi l ngu nhin.
  • Phng sai ca sai s l khng i gia cc quan st. iu ny rt quan trng nh gi cht lng ca m hnh.
  • Cc sai s khng c mi tng quan t hi quy. Thng k Durbin-Watson c bo co bi hi quy pht hin iu ny. Nu n gn bng $2$, th khng c tng quan t hi quy.
  • Cc sai s c phn phi theo phn phi chun. Nu iu ny khng ng, chng ta khng th s dng mt s thng k, chng hn nh kim tra F.

Hi quy tuyn tnh a bin cng yu cu mt gi nh b sung:

  • Khng c mi quan h tuyn tnh chnh xc gia cc bin c lp. Nu khng, khng th gii cho cc h s $\beta_i$ mt cch duy nht, v cng mt phng trnh tuyn tnh c th c biu din theo nhiu cch khc nhau.

Nu c mt mi quan h tuyn tnh gia bt k tp hp no ca cc bin c lp, cn c gi l hip phng sai, chng ta ni rng chng l cc t hp tuyn tnh ca nhau. Trong trng hp chng ph thuc vo nhau theo cch ny, gi tr ca cc h s $\beta_i$ ca chng ta s khng chnh xc cho mt $X_i$ nht nh. Trc gic cho iu ny c th c tm thy trong mt v d cc oan, ni $X_1$ v $X_2$ c hip phng sai 100%. Trong trng hp , hi quy tuyn tnh c th tng ng phn b tng hp h s theo bt k t hp no m khng nh hng n kh nng d on.

$$ 1X_1 + 0X_2 = 0.5X_1 + 0.5X_2 = 0X_1 + 1X_2 $$

Mc d cc h s ca chng ta c th khng m t r rng, m hnh cui cng vn c th chnh xc min l c s ph hp tt gia cc bin c lp v cc bin ph thuc. Thc tin tt nht xy dng mt m hnh m s ph thuc l mt vn l loi b cc bin t m t hn c lin quan n cc bin tt hn. iu ny ci thin m hnh bng cch gim c hi overfitting trong khi a cc c lng $\beta_i$ gn hn vi gi tr thc ca chng.

Nu chng ta xc nhn rng cc gi nh cn thit ca m hnh hi quy c tha mn, chng ta c th yn tm s dng cc thng k c bo co phn tch s ph hp. V d, gi tr $R^2$ cho bit phn trm bin ng tng th ca $Y$ c m hnh gii thch. Tuy nhin, khi thc hin hi quy tuyn tnh a bin, chng ta a thch s dng $R^2$ iu chnh hn, iu ny sa i cho nhng tng nh trong $R^2$ xy ra khi chng ta thm nhiu bin hi quy vo m hnh, ngay c khi chng khng c mi tng quan ng k vi bin ph thuc. $R^2$ iu chnh c nh ngha l

$$ 1 - (1 - R^2)\frac{n-1}{n-k-1} $$

Trong $n$ l s lng quan st v $k$ l s lng bin c lp trong m hnh. Cc thng k hu ch khc bao gm thng k F v sai s chun ca c lng.

V d v La Chn M Hnh

Khi quyt nh m hnh tt nht cho cc bin ph thuc ca bn, c nhiu phng php khc nhau tham kho. Nu bn s dng qu nhiu bin gii thch, bn c nguy c lm qu khp m hnh ca mnh, nhng nu bn s dng qu t, bn c th kt thc vi mt m hnh khng ph hp. Mt trong nhng phng php ni bt nht xc nh m hnh tt nht l hi quy tng bc. Hi quy tng bc bt u t mt m hnh trng v kim tra tng bin ring l, chn ra bin no mang li cht lng m hnh tt nht, thng c o bng AIC hoc BIC (gi tr thp nht l tt nht). Sau , n thm cc bin cn li tng ci mt, kim tra tng t hp bin gii thch tip theo trong hi quy v tnh ton gi tr AIC hoc BIC mi bc. Cui cng ca hi quy, m hnh c cht lng tt nht (theo thc o cho) s c chn v trnh by nh l m hnh tt nht cui cng. Tuy nhin, iu ny c nhng hn ch. N khng kim tra mi t hp bin c th, v vy c th b l m hnh l thuyt tt nht nu mt bin no b loi b sm trong qu trnh thc hin thut ton. Do , hi quy tng bc nn c s dng kt hp vi phn on tt nht ca bn v m hnh.

def forward_aic(response, data):
    # This function will work with pandas dataframes and series

    # Initialize some variables
    explanatory = list(data.columns)
    selected = pd.Series(np.ones(data.shape[0]), name="Intercept")
    current_score, best_new_score = np.inf, np.inf

    # Loop while we haven't found a better model
    while current_score == best_new_score and len(explanatory) != 0:

        scores_with_elements = []
        count = 0

        # For each explanatory variable
        for element in explanatory:
            # Make a set of explanatory variables including our current best and the new one
            tmp = pd.concat([selected, data[element]], axis=1)
            # Test the set
            result = regression.linear_model.OLS(Y, tmp).fit()
            score = result.aic
            scores_with_elements.append((score, element, count))
            count += 1

        # Sort the scoring list
        scores_with_elements.sort(reverse = True)
        # Get the best new variable
        best_new_score, best_element, index = scores_with_elements.pop()
        if current_score > best_new_score:
            # If it's better than the best add it to the set
            explanatory.pop(index)
            selected = pd.concat([selected, data[best_element]],axis=1)
            current_score = best_new_score
    # Return the final model
    model = regression.linear_model.OLS(Y, selected).fit()
    return model
