Gi tr ti Ri ro (VaR) cho mc bao ph $\alpha$ c nh ngha l s tin ti a m chng ta c th k vng s mt vi xc sut $p = 1 - \alpha$. Ni cch khc, trong khng qu $100 \times p \%$ s ngy, chng ta nn k vng s mt nhiu hn VaR. C nhiu cch c tnh VaR v khng c cch no l hon ho. Thc t, bn khng nn t hon ton nim tin vo VaR, n ch yu nhm mc ch gip bn c ci nhn tng quan v mc tn tht c th xy ra trong cc kch bn cc oan khc nhau v cung cp thng tin ny cho nhng ngi chu trch nhim qun l ri ro.

VaR cho mt mc $\alpha$ cao l mt thc o cho cc kt qu ti t nht. V d, mt ngi c th theo di VaR vi $\alpha = 0.999$ hiu cch m mt s kin khng hong 1/1000 c th nh hng n h. Bi v cc phn phi thc t c xu hng phn k v tr nn t nht qun hn khi ta i su vo ui, VaR cc oan nn c tip nhn vi s thn trng.

Mi quan h vi Khong tin cy

i vi nhng ai quen thuc vi khong tin cy, VaR rt ging vi n. tng c gng bao ph mt tp hp cc gi tr c th bng mt khong c xc nh bi $\alpha$ tng t nh cch m VaR c gng bao ph mt tp hp cc tn tht c th. i vi nhng ai khng quen thuc, hy xem bi ging v Khong tin cy.

VaR Lch S (Khng Tham S)

Chng ta s s dng VaR lch s, ci nhn vo phn phi li nhun trc y v s dng n tnh ton phn v $p$. Phn v ny l s tin thua l m bn c th k vng hp l s tri qua vi xc sut $p$, gi nh rng li nhun trong tng lai gn ging vi li nhun trong qu kh. Mt ln na, iu ny khng hon ho v yu cu rng khng c s thay i ch m trong phn phi li nhun thay i. V d, nu khong thi gian lch s ca bn khng bao gm bt k s kin khng hong no, c lng VaR ca bn s thp hn nhiu so vi thc t.

tnh ton VaR lch s cho bao ph $\alpha$, chng ta ch cn ly phn v $100 \times (1 - \alpha)$ ca cc li nhun quan st thp nht v nhn n vi tng gi tr u t ca chng ta.

By gi hy tnh ton VaR ca b 10 ti sn ny. lm iu ny, chng ta cn mt b trng s danh mc u t. Chng ta s bt u tht n gin.

def value_at_risk(value_invested, returns, weights, alpha=0.95, lookback_days=520):
    returns = returns.fillna(0.0)
    # Multiply asset returns by weights to get one weighted portfolio return
    portfolio_returns = returns.iloc[-lookback_days:].dot(weights)
    # Compute the correct percentile loss and multiply by value invested
    return np.percentile(portfolio_returns, 100 * (1-alpha)) * value_invested

VaR Lch S Phn Phi Chun v Phi Tham S

Trng Hp Phn Phi Chun

Mt trng hp c bit ca VaR l khi bn gi nh rng li sut tun theo mt phn phi nht nh thay v c lng phi tham s theo lch s. Trong trng hp ny, VaR chun s ph hp vi d liu ca chng ta, v tt c li sut ca chng ta u c m phng t mt phn phi chun. Chng ta c th kim tra iu ny bng cch s dng Hm Phn Phi Tch Ly (CDF) ca phn phi chun, bng cch tng hp din tch di ng cong chun xc nh kh nng ca nhng gi tr nht nh. Chng ta s s dng CDF ngc, hay PPF, m cho mt xc sut nht nh s cho chng ta bit gi tr no tng ng vi xc sut .

C th, cng thc dng ng cho VaR Phn Phi Chun l

$$VaR_{\alpha}(x) = \mu - \sigma N^{-1}(\alpha)$$

Chng Ti Ni Di V 'Phi Tham S'

Bn s nhn thy rng vic tnh ton VaR mt cch r rng s dng mt khong thi gian lookback. y l mt tham s cho VaR lch s 'phi tham s'. Hy nh rng v khong thi gian lookback nh hng n VaR, nn rt quan trng khi chn mt khong thi gian lookback di VaR hi t. kim tra xem gi tr ca chng ti c v hi t hay khng, hy tin hnh mt th nghim.

Cng phi nh rng ngay c khi mt iu g hi t trong khong thi gian 500 ngy, c th iu ang b qua mt s sp ti chnh xy ra 1000 ngy trc, v do ang b qua d liu quan trng. Mt khc, vic s dng ton b d liu thi gian c th l v ch v l do tnh khng n nh trong phng sai li nhun. C bn l khi phng sai li nhun thay i theo thi gian, cc php o c c th phn nh trng thi khng cn chnh xc na.

Gi tr ri ro iu kin (CVaR)

CVaR l iu m nhiu ngi coi l mt ci tin so vi VaR, v n xem xt hnh dng ca phn phi li nhun. N cng c bit n vi tn gi l Thiu ht k vng (ES), v n l mt k vng v tt c cc khon l c th xy ra ln hn VaR v xc sut c lng tng ng ca chng.

Nu bn cha quen vi cc k vng, c rt nhiu ni dung c sn trc tuyn. Tuy nhin, chng ti s cung cp mt ci nhn tng quan ngn gn.

Gi tr k vng

Gi s bn c mt con xc xc u su mt. Mi s u c xc sut nh nhau. Khi nim v mt k vng, c vit l $\mathrm{E}(X)$, l iu bn nn mong i s xy ra t tt c cc kt qu c th. c c iu ny, bn nhn mi s kin vi xc sut ca s kin v cng chng li, hy coi nh mt trung bnh c trng s xc sut. Vi mt con xc xc, chng ta c:

$$1/6 \times 1 + 1/6 \times 2 + 1/6 \times 3 + 1/6 \times 4 + 1/6 \times 5 + 1/6 \times 6 = 3.5$$

Khi cc xc sut khng bng nhau, n tr nn phc tp hn, v khi cc kt qu l lin tc, chng ta phi s dng tch phn trong cc phng trnh dng ng. y l cng thc cho CVaR.

$$CVaR_{\alpha}(x) \approx \frac{1}{(1-\alpha)} \int_{f(x,y) \geq VaR_{\alpha}(x)} f(x,y)p(y)dy dx$$

In[18]:
def cvar(value_invested, returns, weights, alpha=0.95, lookback_days=520):
    # Call out to our existing function
    var = value_at_risk(value_invested, returns, weights, alpha, lookback_days=lookback_days)
    returns = returns.fillna(0.0)
    portfolio_returns = returns.iloc[-lookback_days:].dot(weights)

    # Get back to a return rather than an absolute loss
    var_pct_loss = var / value_invested

    return value_invested * np.nanmean(portfolio_returns[portfolio_returns < var_pct_loss])

Hy tnh ton CVaR trn d liu ca chng ta v xem n so snh nh th no vi VaR.
