Tp v tr l g?

cp cao, vic chn tp v tr l qu trnh la chn nhm cc chng khon m thut ton ca bn s giao dch. V d, mt thut ton c thit k hot ng vi cc c im ca mt tp v tr bao gm cc c phiu cng ngh c th hot ng rt tt trong tp v tr nhng li tht bi cc lnh vc khc. Th nghim vi cc tp v tr khc nhau bng cch iu chnh cc thnh phn ca chng l mt phn thit yu trong vic pht trin mt chin lc giao dch.

Mc thay i (Turnover)

Mt yu t khc cn xem xt khi thit k mt tp v tr l t l thay i ca v tr . Turnover l mt cch o lng t l thay i ny. Turnover c nh ngha l s lng c phiu vo hoc ra khi tp v tr trong mt khong thi gian c th.

Hy tng tng mt tp v tr c Turnover bng 0. Tp v tr ny s hon ton khng thay i trc nhng bin ng ca th trng. Hn na, nhng c phiu khng ph hp vi tp v tr s khng bao gi b loi b v nhng c phiu nn c a vo s khng bao gi xut hin.

Ngc li, hy tng tng mt tp v tr thay i ton b cc thnh phn ca n mi ngy. Mt thut ton c xy dng trn v tr ny s buc phi bn ton b danh mc u t ca mnh mi ngy. iu ny pht sinh chi ph giao dch, lm gim li nhun.

Khi to ra mt tp v tr, c mt s nh i ni ti gia s tr tr v nhy cm vi th trng.

def calculate_daily_turnover(unstacked):
    return universe.diff().sum(axis=1).iloc[1:]

def plot_daily_turnover(universe):

    # Calculate locations where the inclusion state of an asset changed.
    turnover = calculate_daily_turnover(universe)

    # Write the data to an axis.
    ax = turnover.plot(figsize=(14, 8))

    # Add style to the axis.
    ax.grid(False)
    ax.set_title('Changes per Day')
    ax.set_ylabel('Number of Added or Removed Assets')


def print_daily_turnover_stats(universe):
    turnover = calculate_daily_turnover(universe)
    print('Mean', turnover.mean())
    print('Std', turnover.std())
    print('25%', turnover.quantile(0.25))
    print('50%', turnover.quantile(0.5))
    print('75%', turnover.quantile(0.75))
    print('Min', turnover.min())
    print('Max', turnover.max())
