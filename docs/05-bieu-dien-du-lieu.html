Biu Histogram

Biu histogram l mt hnh nh th hin tn sut ca cc gi tr d liu khc nhau. Bng cch hin th phn b tn sut thng qua cc thanh, n cho php chng ta nhanh chng thy c hu ht cc quan st c nhm li u. Chiu cao ca mi thanh i din cho s lng quan st nm trong mi khong. Bn c th ngh v biu histogram nh mt Hm Mt Xc sut (PDF) thc nghim v ri rc.

In[6]:
# Plot a histogram using 20 bins
plt.hist(data['FPT'], bins=20)
plt.xlabel('Price')
plt.ylabel('Number of Days Observed')
plt.title('Frequency Distribution of FPT Prices, 2024');

Biu Phn Phi Li Nhun

Trong lnh vc ti chnh, him khi chng ta xem xt phn phi ca gi c. Nguyn nhn l do gi c khng n nh v thng xuyn bin ng. Thay vo , chng ta s s dng li nhun hng ngy. Hy cng th nghim iu ngay by gi.

In[7]:
# Remove the first element because percent change from nothing to something is NaN
R = data['FPT'].pct_change()[1:]

# Plot a histogram using 20 bins
plt.hist(R, bins=20)
plt.xlabel('Return')
plt.ylabel('Number of Days Observed')
plt.title('Frequency Distribution of FPT Returns, 2024');

Biu trn cho thy, v d, rng li nhun hng ngy ca FPT cao hn 0,06 vo cha n 5 ngy trong nm 2024. Lu rng chng ta hon ton loi b cc ngy tng ng vi nhng li nhun ny.

QUAN TRNG: Cng lu rng iu ny khng c ngha l cc li nhun trong tng lai s c cng phn phi.

Biu Tch ly (CDF c lng ri rc)

Mt cch khc hin th d liu l s dng hm phn phi tch ly, trong chiu cao ca mt ct biu th s lng quan st nm trong ct hoc trong mt trong cc ct trc . Biu ny lun lun khng gim v bn khng th c s lng quan st m. S la chn biu ph thuc vo thng tin m bn quan tm.

In[9]:
# Remove the first element because percent change from nothing to something is NaN
R = data['FPT'].pct_change()[1:]

# Plot a histogram using 20 bins
plt.hist(R, bins=20, cumulative=True)
plt.xlabel('Return')
plt.ylabel('Number of Days Observed')
plt.title('Cumulative Distribution of FPT Returns, 2024');

Biu phn tn (Scatter plot)

Biu phn tn rt hu ch trc quan ha mi quan h gia hai tp d liu. Chng ta s dng hai tp d liu c s tng ng no , chng hn nh ngy m php o c thc hin. Mi im trn biu i din cho hai gi tr tng ng t hai tp d liu. Tuy nhin, chng ta khng v ngy m cc php o c thc hin.

ng Bao Gi Gi nh "Cc iu Kin l Khng i"

Mt ln na, khi s dng th trc quan ha d liu, ng gi nh rng bn c th kim tra mt gi thuyt ch bng cch nhn vo th. Cng ng gi nh rng v mt phn phi hoc xu hng tng ng, nn n vn cn ng. Ni chung, cn c s xc thc tinh vi v cn thn hn nhiu kim tra xem cc m hnh c ng hay khng. th ch yu hu ch khi bn ban u quyt nh cch m cc m hnh ca bn nn hot ng.

Lu : Ti liu ny mang tnh cht chia s kin thc khoa hc d liu. Cc m chng khon xut hin trong ti liu ch mang tnh cht minh ho, khng khuyn ngh u t. Chng ti khng chu trch nhim trc mi ri ro no do s dng ti liu ny.
