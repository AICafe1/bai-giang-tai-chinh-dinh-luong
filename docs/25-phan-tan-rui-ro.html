By gi Hy Gii Thch Bng Ton Hc Thay V Hnh nh

Mt trong nhng kha cnh chnh ca l thuyt danh mc u t hin i l vic kt hp nhiu ti sn vo mt danh mc u t c th gim thiu ri ro tng th ca ton b gi. V chng ta i din cho bin ng ca mt ti sn bng lch chun ca n, chng ta c th d dng ch ra iu ny bng ton hc.

Gi s rng chng ta c hai ti sn trong mt danh mc u t, $S_1$ v $S_2$, vi trng s $\omega_1$ v $\omega_2$ sao cho $\omega_1 + \omega_2 = 1$. Gi danh mc u t l $P$ v gi s rng $S_1$ v $S_2$ c trung bnh v lch chun ln lt l $\mu_1, \sigma_1$ v $\mu_2, \sigma_2$. Chng ta c th tnh ton gi tr ca $P$ mt cch d dng.

$$ P = \omega_1 S_1 + \omega_2 S_2 $$

By gi, chng ta t $\mu_P$ l li sut ca danh mc u t $P$. Tht n gin tnh ton li sut k vng ca danh mc u t ny:

$$ E[\mu_P] = E[\omega_1 \mu_1 + \omega_2 \mu_2] = \omega_1 E[\mu_1] + \omega_2 E[\mu_2] $$

Nh bn c th thy, li sut k vng ca ton b danh mc u t c th c xc nh trc tip bng cch s dng li sut k vng ca cc ti sn trong danh mc u t cng nh trng s tng ng ca chng. Tng t, chng ta c th s dng cc c im ny xc nh ri ro tng th ca danh mc u t, $\sigma_p$. u tin, chng ta tnh ton phng sai ca danh mc u t, $\sigma_p^2 = VAR[P]$. Sau , chng ta ni rng h s tng quan gia $S_1$ v $S_2$ l $COR[S_1,S_2] = \frac{COV[S_1,S_2]}{\sigma_1\sigma_2} = \rho_{12}$. Cc php tnh sau s theo sau:

\begin{eqnarray} \sigma_p^2 &=& VAR[P] \\ &=& VAR[\omega_1 S_1 + \omega_2 S_2] \\ &=& VAR[\omega_1 S_1] + VAR[\omega_2 S_2] + COV[\omega_1 S_1,\omega_2 S_2] \\ &=& \omega_1^2 VAR[S_1] + \omega_2^2 VAR[S_2] + 2\omega_1\omega_2 COV[S_1,S_2] \\ &=& \omega_1^2 \sigma_1^2 + \omega_2^2 \sigma_2^2 + 2\rho_{12}\omega_1\omega_2\sigma_1\sigma_2 \end{eqnarray}

Li ch ca cc ln cc b sung

Chng ta c th m rng tng ny sang mt danh mc gm $n$ chng khon mt cch kh d dng. Cng nhiu ti sn bn a vo danh mc ca mnh, ri ro tng th ca bn s cng thp. y l dng tng qut cho phng sai ca danh mc u t trong trng hp bn c $n$ ti sn:

$$ \sigma_p^2 = \sum_i \omega_i^2 \sigma_i^2 + \sum_i\sum_{j\neq i} \omega_i\omega_j\sigma_i\sigma_j\rho_{ij}, \ i, j \in \lbrace 1,\ldots, n\rbrace $$

Cc li ch ny c th c tng cng bng cch m bo rng cc ti sn ca bn c lp vi nhau. Khi hai ti sn c lp, chng khng tng quan, tc l $\rho_{ij}=0$. S tng quan gia mi cp ti sn ng mt vai tr rt quan trng trong cc php tnh ca chng ta xc nh phng sai ca mt danh mc u t. S tng quan cao hn gia cc ti sn, th chng ta cn phi bao gm nhiu ti sn hn gim ri ro ca mnh vi mt lng tng ng.

# Generate an almost entirely random portfolio
# Only constraint is that an asset's variance
def generate_portfolio(n = 5):
    assets = np.random.normal(100, 20, n)
    weights = np.random.uniform(0, 1, n)
    weights = weights/sum(weights)
    returns = np.random.normal(5, 2, n) # Say that 5 is the average return for our assets
    # Generate covariance matrix for assets
    cov_matrix = np.ndarray(shape = (n, n))
    std_dev = np.zeros(n)
    for i in range(n):
        for j in range(i + 1):
            if j == i:
                std_dev[i] = returns[i]/100
                cov_matrix[i][j] = std_dev[i]**2
            else:
                cov_matrix[i][j] = np.random.uniform(-1, 1)
                cov_matrix[j][i] = cov_matrix[i][j]
    # Ensures that the covariance matrix is symmetric
    # Serves the double purpose of squaring the volatility (already present in the matrix) so we get variance
    return weights, assets, returns, std_dev, cov_matrix
