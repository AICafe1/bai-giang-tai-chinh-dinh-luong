Bin ng lu nay lun l mt vn kh khn i vi cc nh u t trn th trng. Vic o lng bin ng mt cch chnh xc s cho php m hnh ha li nhun mt cch chnh xc hn v to ra cc khon u t n nh hn, dn n li nhun ln hn, nhng d on bin ng mt cch chnh xc l mt bi ton kh.

o Lng Bin ng

Bin ng cn phi c ci nhn hng ti tng lai v mang tnh d on a ra nhng quyt nh thng minh. Tht khng may, vic ch n gin ly lch chun lch s ca li sut ca mt ti sn c nhn l khng khi chng ta xem xt nhu cu v bn vng trong tng lai. Khi chng ta m rng vn n mc cn d on bin ng cho nhiu ti sn, iu ny tr nn kh khn hn na.

m hnh ha cch m mt danh mc u t thay i mt cch tng th, iu quan trng l khng ch nhn vo bin ng ca tng ti sn trong danh mc, m cn phi xem xt cc hip phng sai theo cp gia mi ti sn c lin quan. Mi quan h gia hai hoc nhiu ti sn cung cp nhng thng tin qu gi v mt con ng hng ti vic gim thiu bin ng tng th ca danh mc u t. Mt s lng ln ti sn vi hip phng sai thp s m bo rng chng gim hoc tng mt cch c lp vi nhau. Cc ti sn c lp s c t tc ng hn n bin ng ca danh mc u t ca chng ta v chng mang li s a dng thc s v gip chng ta trnh ri ro tp trung v th.

Hip phng sai

Trong thng k v xc sut, hip phng sai l mt thc o s bin i chung ca hai bin ngu nhin. Khi cc bin ngu nhin th hin hnh vi tng t, thng s c hip phng sai cao gia chng. V mt ton hc, chng ta biu din hip phng sai ca X i vi Y nh sau:

$$ COV(X, Y) = E[(X - E[X])(Y - E[Y])]$$

Lu rng nu chng ta ly hip phng sai ca $X$ vi chnh n, chng ta s nhn c:

$$ COV(X, X) = E[(X - E[X])(X - E[X])] = E[(X - E[X])^2] = VAR(X) $$

Chng ta c th s dng hip phng sai nh lng s tng ng gia cc ti sn khc nhau theo cch tng t. Nu hai ti sn c hip phng sai cao, chng thng s c hnh vi ging nhau. Cc ti sn c hip phng sai c bit cao c th thay th ln nhau.

Ma trn hip phng sai cu thnh nn tng ca l thuyt Danh mc Hin i (MPT). MPT tp trung vo vic ti a ha li nhun cho mt mc ri ro nht nh, lm cho cc phng php m chng ta s dng c lng ri ro tr nn thit yu. Chng ta s dng hip phng sai nh lng ri ro chung ca cc ti sn, hnh thnh cch chng ta nhn nhn ri ro ca ton b danh mc. iu quan trng l u t vo cc ti sn c hip phng sai cp cao s cung cp t s a dng ha v s bin ng ca chng lin quan cht ch vi nhau.

from statsmodels import regression
import statsmodels.api as sm
def linreg(X,Y):
    # Running the linear regression
    X = sm.add_constant(X)
    model = regression.linear_model.OLS(Y, X).fit()
    a = model.params[0]
    b = model.params[1]
    X = X[:, 1]

    # Return summary of the regression and plot results
    X2 = np.linspace(X.min(), X.max(), 100)
    Y_hat = X2 * b + a
    plt.scatter(X, Y, alpha=0.3) # Plot the raw data
    plt.plot(X2, Y_hat, 'r', alpha=0.9);  # Add the regression line, colored in red
    plt.xlabel('X Value')
    plt.ylabel('Y Value')
    return model.summary()

Ma trn hip phng sai

Khi s lng ti sn m chng ta quan tm tng ln, s chiu ca ma trn hip phng sai m t mi quan h ca chng cng tng theo. Nu chng ta ly hip phng sai gia $N$ ti sn, chng ta s c c mt ma trn hip phng sai kch thc $N \times N$. iu ny cho php chng ta din t mt cch hiu qu mi quan h gia nhiu mng cng mt lc. Tng t nh trng hp n gin $2\times 2$, phn cho th $i$ l phng sai ca ti sn th $i$ v cc gi tr ti $(i, j)$ v $(j, i)$ cp n hip phng sai gia ti sn $i$ v ti sn $j$. Chng ta th hin iu ny bng k hiu sau:

$$ \Sigma = \left[\begin{matrix} VAR(X_1) & COV(X_1, X_2) & \cdots & COV(X_1, X_N) \\ COV(X_2, X_0) & VAR(X_2) & \cdots & COV(X_2, X_N) \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ COV(X_N, X_1) & COV(X_N, X_2) & \cdots & VAR(X_N) \end{matrix}\right] $$

Khi c gng tm tng quan ca nhiu ti sn, chng ta nhanh chng nhn ra l do ti sao k hiu ma trn li c a chung hn.

Ti sao tt c nhng iu ny li quan trng?

Chng ta o lng hip phng sai ca cc ti sn trong danh mc u t ca mnh m bo rng chng ta c mt bc tranh chnh xc v cc ri ro lin quan n vic nm gi nhng ti sn cng nhau. Chng ta mun phn b vn ca mnh gia cc ti sn ny mt cch sao cho gim thiu s tip xc vi cc ri ro lin quan n tng ti sn ring r v trung ha s tip xc vi ri ro h thng. iu ny c thc hin thng qua qu trnh ti u ha danh mc u t. Cc quy trnh ti u ha danh mc u t s tri qua chnh xc qu trnh ny, tm ra trng s ph hp cho tng ti sn da trn cc ri ro ca n. Ti u ha trung bnh-phng sai, mt yu t ch cht ca MPT, thc hin chnh xc iu ny.

Vic c lng ma trn hip phng sai tr nn rt quan trng khi s dng cc phng php da vo n, v chng ta khng th bit c cc mi quan h thng k thc s nm sau cc ti sn m chng ta chn. S n nh v chnh xc ca nhng c lng ny rt cn thit c c trng s n nh phn nh ri ro v nh ca chng ta.

Cc gi tr cc tr phn ng mnh m hn vi nhng thay i, v khi cc gi tr cc tr ca hip phng sai nhy ma, cc b ti u ha ca chng ta b xo trn, dn n nhng trng s khng nht qun. y l mt vn khi chng ta c gng thc hin nhiu ln cc c lp vo nhiu ti sn ci thin s tip xc ri ro ca mnh thng qua a dng ha. Ngay c khi chng ta c nhiu yu t thi gian hn s lng ti sn m chng ta giao dch, chng ta vn c th gp phi cc vn , v thnh phn thi gian c th tri qua nhiu ch khc nhau, dn n cc ma trn hip phng sai vn khng chnh xc.

Cc c Lng Shrinkage

Khi nim shrinkage bt ngun t nhu cu c cc ma trn phng sai n nh. Cch c bn m chng ta "shrink" mt ma trn l gim cc gi tr cc tr ca ma trn phng sai mu bng cch ko chng gn hn n trung tm. Thc t, chng ta ly mt t hp tuyn tnh ca ma trn phng sai mu v mt mng hng s i din cho trung tm.

Cho mt ma trn phng sai mu, $\textbf{S}$, gi tr trung bnh, $\mu$, v hng s shrinkage $\delta$, ma trn phng sai c lng c shrink li c nh ngha ton hc nh sau:

$$(1 - \delta)\textbf{S} + \delta\mu\textbf{1}$$

Chng ta gii hn $\delta$ sao cho $0 \leq \delta \leq 1$, lm cho iu ny tr thnh mt trung bnh c trng s gia ma trn phng sai mu v ma trn gi tr trung bnh. Gi tr ti u ca $\delta$ c gii nhiu ln. i vi mc ch ca chng ta, chng ta s s dng cng thc ca Ledoit v Wolf.

c Lng Ledoit-Wolf

Trong bi bo ca h, Ledoit v Wolf xut mt $\delta$ ti u:

$$\hat\delta^* \max\{0, \min\{\frac{\hat\kappa}{T},1\}\}$$

$\hat\kappa$ c mt cng thc ton hc m vt qu phm vi ca bi ging ny, nhng bn c th tm thy nh ngha ca n trong bi bo.

c lng Ledoit-Wolf l c lng phng sai vng chc s dng $\hat\delta^*$ ti u ny shrink ma trn phng sai mu. Chng ta c th thc hin n trc tip t scikit-learn s dng d dng.

Tnh Ton Li

Chng ta c th nh lng s khc bit gia cc c lng trong mu v ngoi mu bng cch ly chnh lch tuyt i tng phn cho hai ma trn. Chng ta biu din iu ny mt cch ton hc nh sau:

$$ \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} |a_i - b_i| $$

u tin, chng ta tnh ton c lng ngoi mu v sau so snh.
