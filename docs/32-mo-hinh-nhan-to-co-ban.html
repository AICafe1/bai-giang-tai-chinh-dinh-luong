M Hnh Cc Nhn T C Bn L G?

D liu c bn cp n cc ch s v t l o lng cc c im ti chnh ca cc cng ty c ly t cc bo co cng khai m cc cng ty ny thc hin, chng hn nh bo co thu nhp v bng cn i k ton ca h. V d v cc nhn t c rt ra t cc ti liu ny bao gm vn ha th trng, tng trng thu nhp rng v dng tin.

D liu c bn c th c s dng theo nhiu cch, mt trong s l xy dng mt m hnh nhn t tuyn tnh. Vi mt tp hp cc nhn t c bn $k$, chng ta c th i din cho li nhun ca mt ti sn, $R_t$, nh sau:

$$R_t = \alpha_t + \beta_{t, F_1}F_1 + \beta_{t, F_2}F_2 + ... + \beta_{t, F_k}F_k + \epsilon_t$$

Trong mi $F_j$ i din cho mt dng li nhun nhn t c bn. Cc dng li nhun ny n t cc danh mc u t m gi tr ca n c xc nh t nhn t tng ng.

M hnh nhn t c bn c gng xc nh cc c im nh hng n ri ro v li nhun ca mt ti sn. Phn kh khn nht trong vic ny l xc nh nhn t no s c s dng. Nhiu nghin cu c thc hin xc nh cc nhn t quan trng, v iu lm mi th tr nn kh khn hn l vic pht hin ra mt nhn t quan trng thng dn n li th ca n b khai thc! y l mt trong nhng l do ti sao cc m hnh nhn t c bn, v cc m hnh nhn t tuyn tnh ni chung, li ph bin trong ti chnh hin i. Khi bn tm thy cc nhn t quan trng, bn cn tnh ton mc tip xc m dng li nhun ca ti sn c vi tng nhn t. iu ny tng t nh vic tnh ton cc khon b ri ro c tho lun trong bi ging CAPM.

Khi s dng d liu c bn, chng ta gp phi vn c nhng nhn t c th khng d dng so snh do n v v ln khc nhau ca chng. gii quyt iu ny, chng ta p dng hai phng php khc nhau a d liu ln cng mt thang o - xy dng danh mc u t so snh cc dng li nhun v chun ha gi tr ca cc nhn t.

Phng Php Mt: Xy Dng Danh Mc u T

Phng php u tin bao gm vic s dng d liu c bn nh mt h thng xp hng v to ra mt danh mc u t c phiu Long-Short da trn tng nhn t. Sau , chng ta s dng cc dng li nhun lin quan n mi danh mc lm cc nhn t m hnh ca mnh.

Mt trong nhng v d ni ting nht ca phng php ny l m hnh Fama-French. M hnh Fama-French, v sau l m hnh bn nhn t Carhart, thm vn ha th trng, t l gi tr s sch trn gi, v ng lc (momentum) vo m hnh CAPM (m hnh m ch bao gm ri ro th trng).

Lch s cho thy, mt s nhm c phiu c coi l c hiu sut tt hn th trng, c th l nhng c phiu c vn ha th trng nh, t l gi tr s sch trn gi cao v nhng c phiu hot ng tt trong qu kh (tc l, chng c ng lc). Thc nghim, Fama & French pht hin ra rng li nhun ca nhng loi c phiu ny thng tt hn nhng g c d on bi ng th trng chng khon ca CAPM.

nm bt nhng hin tng ny, chng ta s s dng cc nhn t to ra mt h thng xp hng s c s dng trong vic to ra cc danh mc u t c phiu Long-Short. Cc nhn t s l $SMB$, o lng li nhun vt tri ca cc cng ty c vn ha th trng nh so vi ln, $HML$, o lng li nhun vt tri ca cc cng ty c t l gi tr s sch trn gi cao so vi thp, $MOM$, o lng li nhun vt tri ca nhng c phiu chin thng thng trc so vi nhng c phiu thua thng trc, v $EXMRKT$, l mt thc o ri ro th trng.

Ni chung, phng php ny c th c s dng nh mt m hnh nh gi ti sn hoc phng v cho cc danh mc u t ca chng ta. Ci sau s dng hi quy Fama-Macbeth tnh ton cc khon tin ri ro, nh trnh by trong bi ging CAPM. Vic phng v c th t c thng qua mt hi quy tuyn tnh ca li nhun danh mc trn li nhun t cc danh mc u t nhn t Long-Short. Di y l v d cho c hai.

tickers = np.array(universe('VN100')[:'2023'].iloc[-1].dropna().index)

start_date, end_date = '2024-01-01', '2024-12-31'

# Prices Data
prices = get_prices(*tickers, start_date='2023-12-01', end_date=end_date, field=['close', 'share', 'unadjusted'])
market_caps = 1000 * prices.xs('unadjusted', axis=1, level=1) * prices.xs('share', axis=1, level=1)
closes = prices.xs('close', axis=1, level=1).ffill()
returns = closes.pct_change(fill_method=None)[start_date:]

# Fundamental Data
book_value = fin_std({'_': 302, 'bs26857059': '309+308', 'bs7037699336': 4, 'bs1683709501': '302+303'})
BOOK = pd.DataFrame()
for ticker in tickers:
  bsq = get_fin_stmt(ticker, 'bsq', True)
  bsd = fin_stmt_date_idx(bsq)
  bv = book_value(bsd)
  bv = bv[~bv.index.duplicated(keep='last')]
  BOOK = BOOK.join(bv.to_frame(ticker), how='outer')

book_to_price = date_reindex(BOOK, market_caps.index) / market_caps

# Size
cap_rank = market_caps.rank(axis=1, ascending=False)
R_biggest = returns[cap_rank < 31].mean(axis=1)
R_smallest = returns[cap_rank > 70].mean(axis=1)

# Value
value_rank = book_to_price.rank(axis=1, ascending=False)
R_highpb = returns[value_rank < 31].mean(axis=1)
R_lowpb = returns[value_rank > 70].mean(axis=1)

# Momentum monthly
momentum = (closes - closes.shift(20)) / closes.shift(20)
momentum_rank = momentum.rank(axis=1, ascending=False)
R_top = returns[momentum_rank < 31].mean(axis=1)
R_bottom = returns[momentum_rank > 70].mean(axis=1)

# Risk free and benchmark
R_F = .05/252
M = get_prices('VN100', frequency='index', end_date=end_date).VN100.pct_change()[start_date:]

# Defining our final factors
EXMRKT = M - R_F
SMB = R_smallest - R_biggest # small minus big
HML = R_highpb - R_lowpb # high minus low
MOM = R_top - R_bottom # momentum

kim tra tnh chun, chng ta s thc hin bi kim tra Jarque-Bera, bi kim tra ny xem liu lch v nhn ca d liu ca chng ta c ph hp vi phn phi chun hay khng. Theo tiu chun, chng ta s bc b gi thuyt null rng d liu ca chng ta phn phi chun nu gi tr p-value ca chng ta di mc tin cy 5%.

kim tra tnh khng ng nht phng sai, chng ta s thc hin bi kim tra Breush-Pagan, bi kim tra ny xem liu phng sai ca cc sai s trong hi quy tuyn tnh c lin quan n cc gi tr ca cc bin c lp hay khng. Trong trng hp ny, gi thuyt nyll ca chng ta l d liu l ng nht phng sai.

T tng quan c kim tra bng cch s dng thng k Durbin-Watson, thng k ny xem xt mi quan h tr gia cc sai s trong mt hi quy. iu ny s cho bn mt s gia 0 v 4, vi 2 c ngha l khng c t tng quan.

Phng php Hai: Chun ha Gi tr Yu t

Mt phng php khc l chun ha gi tr yu t cho mi ngy v xem chng d on li nhun ca ngy hm nh th no. iu ny cn c gi l phn tch yu t theo mt ct ngang (cross-sectional). Chng ta thc hin iu ny bng cch tnh gi tr yu t chun ha $b_{a,j}$ cho mi ti sn $a$ theo cch sau.

$$b_{a,j} = \frac{F_{a,j} - \mu_{F_j}}{\sigma_{F_j}}$$

$F_{a,j}$ l gi tr ca yu t $j$ cho ti sn $a$ trong thi gian ny, $\mu_{F_j}$ l gi tr trung bnh ca yu t trn tt c cc ti sn, v $\sigma_{F_j}$ l lch chun ca cc gi tr yu t trn tt c cc ti sn. Lu rng chng ta ch ang tnh mt im z lm cho cc gi tr yu t c th ca ti sn c th so snh c gia cc yu t khc nhau.

Cc ngoi l cho cng thc ny l cc bin nh phn, c t thnh 1 cho ng v 0 cho sai. Mt v d l thnh vin ngnh: h s cho bit liu ti sn c thuc v ngnh hay khng.

Sau khi chng ta tnh ton tt c cc im s chun ha trong thi gian $t$, chng ta c th c lng li nhun ca yu t $j$ l $F_{j,t}$, s dng hi quy theo mt ct ngang (tc l ti mi bc thi gian, chng ta thc hin mt hi quy s dng cc phng trnh cho tt c cc ti sn). C th, khi chng ta c li nhun cho mi ti sn $R_{a,t}$, v cc h s yu t chun ha $b_{a,j}$, chng ta xy dng m hnh sau v c lng cc $F_j$ v $a_t$

$$R_{a,t} = a_t + b_{a,F_1}F_1 + b_{a, F_2}F_2 + \dots + b_{a, F_K}F_K$$

Bn c th ngh v iu ny nh vic ct theo hng khc t phn tch u tin, v by gi li nhun yu t l cc n s cn c gii, trong khi ban u cc h s l cc n s. Mt cch khc ngh v n l bn ang xc nh yu t ny c kh nng d on li nhun vo ngy hm nh th no, v do bn c th to ra bao nhiu li nhun t yu t .
