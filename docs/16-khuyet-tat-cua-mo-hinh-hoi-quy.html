Nhng khuyt tt ca cc m hnh hi quy

By Evgenia "Jenny" Nitishinskaya and Delaney Mackenzie

Edited by AI Cafe

Khi s dng hi quy ph hp vi m hnh d liu ca chng ta, cc gi nh ca phn tch hi quy phi c tha mn m bo c lng tham s tt v thng k ph hp chnh xc. Chng ta mong mun cc tham s phi:

  • khng thin lch (gi tr k vng qua nhiu mu l gi tr thc)
  • nht qun (tin gn n gi tr thc vi nhiu mu), v
  • hiu qu (bin thin c ti thiu ha)

Di y, chng ta s iu tra cch m nhng gi nh ny c th b vi phm v nh hng ca chng n cc tham s v thng k ph hp. Chng ta s s dng cc phng trnh hi quy tuyn tnh mt bin cho cc v d, nhng nhng xem xt tng t cng p dng cho cc m hnh khc. Chng ta cng s gi nh rng m hnh ca chng ta c xc nh ng; tc l, hnh thc chc nng m chng ta chn l hp l. Chng ta s tho lun v cc li xc nh m hnh cng vi cc vi phm gi nh v cc vn khc m chng gy ra trong mt bi ging khc.

Tp trung vo cc phn d (residuals)

Thay v tp trung vo vic xy dng m hnh, bn c th thu thp c mt lng thng tin ln t cc phn d (li) ca mnh. M hnh ca bn c th rt phc tp v kh phn tch, nhng min l bn c cc d on v gi tr quan st, bn c th tnh ton c cc phn d. Khi bn c cc phn d, bn c th thc hin nhiu bi kim tra thng k.

Nu cc phn d ca bn khng tun theo mt phn phi nht nh (thng l phn phi chun, nhng ph thuc vo m hnh ca bn), th bn bit rng c iu g khng n v bn nn quan tm n chnh xc ca cc d on ca mnh.

Phn d khng phn phi chun

Nu thnh phn li khng phi l phn phi chun, th cc kim nh v ngha thng k ca chng ta s khng chnh xc. May mn thay, nh l gii hn trung tm cho chng ta bit rng, i vi cc mu d liu ln, cc phn phi h s s gn nh l phn phi chun ngay c khi cc li khng phi nh vy. Do , phn tch ca chng ta vn s hp l cho cc tp d liu ln.

Kim tra tnh chun

Mt bi kim tra tt cho tnh chun l bi kim tra Jarque-Bera. N c mt trin khai bng python ti statsmodels.stats.stattools.jarque_bera, v chng ta s s dng n thng xuyn trong ti liu ny.

Hy lun kim tra tnh chun!

N cc k d dng v c th gip bn tit kim rt nhiu thi gian.

Kim tra Heteroskedasticity

Bn c th kim tra heteroskedasticity bng mt vi bi kim tra, chng ta s s dng bi kim tra Breush Pagan t th vin statsmodels. Chng ta cng s kim tra tnh chun, iu ny trong trng hp ny cng s pht hin ra s k l trong trng hp th hai. Mc d vy, c th c cc phn d c phn phi chun m cng c tnh heteroskedastic, v vy c hai bi kim tra u phi c thc hin m bo.

Heteroskedasticity nh hng n phn tch ca chng ta nh th no? Tnh hung vn , c gi l heteroskedasticity iu kin, l khi phng sai li c mi tng quan vi cc bin c lp nh nu trn. iu ny lm cho bi kim tra F xc nh ngha ca hi quy v cc bi kim tra t cho ngha ca cc h s ring l tr nn khng ng tin cy. Thng th iu ny dn n vic c lng qu mc ngha ca s ph hp.

Kim tra Breusch-Pagan v kim tra White c th c s dng pht hin heteroskedasticity iu kin. Nu chng ta nghi ng rng hiu ng ny tn ti, chng ta c th iu chnh m hnh ca mnh c gng sa cha cho n. Mt phng php l bnh phng ti thiu tng qut, yu cu mt s thay i th cng ca phng trnh gc. Mt phng php khc l tnh ton sai s chun mnh m, iu chnh cc thng k ph hp tnh n heteroskedasticity. statsmodels c th tnh ton sai s chun mnh m; lu s khc bit trong cc thng k bn di.

Tng quan chui ca li

Mt vn ph bin v nghim trng l khi cc li c tng quan gia cc quan st (c gi l tng quan chui hoc t tng quan). iu ny c th xy ra, chng hn, khi mt s im d liu c lin quan hoc khi chng ta s dng d liu chui thi gian vi nhng bin ng theo chu k. Nu mt trong cc bin c lp ph thuc vo cc gi tr trc ca bin ph thuc - chng hn nh khi n bng gi tr ca bin ph thuc trong k trc - hoc nu vic ch nh m hnh sai dn n t tng quan, th cc c lng h s s khng nht qun v do khng hp l. Ngc li, cc c lng tham s s hp l, nhng cc thng k ph hp s khng chnh xc. V d, nu tng quan l dng, chng ta s c cc thng k F v t b thi phng, dn n vic chng ta c lng qu cao v s quan trng ca m hnh.

Nu cc li l ng phng sai, chng ta c th kim tra s t tng quan bng cch s dng kim tra Durbin-Watson, iu ny c bo co thun tin trong tm tt hi quy trong statsmodels.

Newey-West

Newey-West l mt phng php tnh ton phng sai m tnh n s t tng quan. Mt php tnh phng sai n gin s thc s to ra cc sai s chun khng chnh xc khi c s t tng quan.

Chng ta c th c gng thay i phng trnh hi quy loi b s tng quan chui. Mt cch khc phc n gin hn l iu chnh cc sai s chun bng cch s dng mt phng php thch hp v s dng cc gi tr iu chnh kim tra s ngha. Di y, chng ta s dng phng php Newey-West t statsmodels tnh ton cc sai s chun iu chnh cho cc h s. Chng cao hn so vi cc gi tr ban u c bo co bi hi quy, iu ny l iu chng ta mong i i vi cc sai s c tng quan dng.

a tng quan

Khi s dng nhiu bin c lp, vic kim tra a tng quan l rt quan trng; tc l, mt mi quan h tuyn tnh gn ng gia cc bin c lp, chng hn nh $$ X_2 \approx 5 X_1 - X_3 + 4.5 $$

Vi a tng quan, vic xc nh nh hng c lp ca tng bin l kh khn, v chng ta c th thay i cc h s theo mi quan h tuyn tnh m khng lm thay i m hnh. Nh vi cc bin thc s khng cn thit, iu ny thng khng lm gim chnh xc ca m hnh, nhng s lm m phn tch ca chng ta. C th, cc h s c lng s c sai s chun ln. Cc h s cng s khng cn i din cho nh hng ring ca tng bin, v vi a tng quan chng ta khng th thay i mt bin trong khi gi cc bin khc c nh.

S tng quan cao gia cc bin c lp l du hiu ca a tng quan. Tuy nhin, iu ny khng , v chng ta mun pht hin s tng quan gia mt trong cc bin v mt t hp tuyn tnh ca cc bin cn li. Nu chng ta c R-bnh phng cao nhng t-statistics thp trn cc h s (s ph hp l tt nhng cc h s khng c c lng chnh xc) chng ta c th nghi ng v a tng quan. gii quyt vn , chng ta c th loi b mt trong cc bin c lp lin quan n mi quan h tuyn tnh.

V d, vic s dng hai ch s chng khon lm bin c lp ca chng ta c kh nng dn n a tng quan. Di y chng ta c th thy rng vic loi b mt trong s ci thin t-statistics m khng lm gim R-bnh phng.

Mt iu quan trng khc cn xc nh y l bin no c th l bin nguyn nhn. Nu chng ta gi thuyt rng th trng nh hng n c FPT v HPG, th th trng l bin m chng ta nn s dng trong m hnh d on ca mnh.
