Phng v Beta (Beta Hedging)

By Evgenia "Jenny" Nitishinskaya and Delaney Granizo-Mackenzie with example algorithms by David Edwards

Edited by AI Cafe

M hnh cc nhn t

M hnh cc nhn t l mt cch gii thch li nhun ca mt ti sn thng qua mt t hp tuyn tnh ca li nhun ca cc ti sn khc. Dng tng qut ca mt m hnh cc nhn t l

$$Y = \alpha + \beta_1 X_1 + \beta_2 X_2 + \dots + \beta_n X_n$$

iu ny c v quen thuc, v n chnh xc l m hnh m hi quy tuyn tnh. Cc $X$ cng c th l ch s thay v ti sn. Mt v d c th l c lng ca mt nh phn tch.

Beta l g?

Beta ca mt ti sn i vi mt ti sn khc ch n gin l $\beta$ t m hnh trn. V d, nu chng ta hi quy FPT so vi VNINDEX s dng m hnh $Y_{FPT} = \alpha + \beta X$, th beta ca FPT i vi VNINDEX s l beta . Nu chng ta s dng m hnh $Y_{FPT} = \alpha + \beta X_{VNINDEX} + \beta X_{VCB}$, th by gi chng ta c hai beta, mt l mc tip xc ca FPT vi VNINDEX v mt l mc tip xc ca FPT vi VCB.

Thng th "beta" s cp n mc tip xc beta ca mt c phiu vi VNINDEX. Chng ti s s dng n vi ngha tr khi c ch nh khc.

# Let's define everything in familiar regression terms
X = r_b.values # Get just the values, ignore the timestamps
Y = r_a.values

def linreg(x,y):
    # We add a constant so that we can also fit an intercept (alpha) to the model
    # This just adds a column of 1s to our data
    x = sm.add_constant(x)
    model = regression.linear_model.OLS(y,x).fit()
    # Remove the constant now that we're done
    x = x[:, 1]
    return model.params[0], model.params[1]

alpha, beta = linreg(X,Y)
print('alpha: ' + str(alpha))
print('beta: ' + str(beta))

Ri Ro

Ni chung, beta ny lin quan n khi nim v mc ri ro m bn chp nhn khi nm gi mt ti sn. Nu mt ti sn c nhy beta cao i vi VNINDEX, th trong khi n s hot ng rt tt khi th trng tng, n s hot ng rt km khi th trng gim. Beta cao tng ng vi ri ro u c cao. Bn ang thc hin mt ln cc c tnh bin ng ln hn.

Nhiu nh u t t chc nh gi cao cc chin lc c nhy beta khng ng k i vi nhiu yu t cng tt. iu ny c ngha l tt c cc khon li nhun trong mt chin lc nm trong phn $\alpha$ ca m hnh v c lp vi cc yu t khc. iu ny rt c mong i, v n c ngha l chin lc ny khng ph thuc vo iu kin th trng. N s kim tin tt nh nhau trong mt cuc khng hong cng nh trong mt th trng tng trng. Nhng chin lc ny l nhng chin lc hp dn nht i vi nhng c nhn c qu tin ln nh qu t thin v qu ti sn quc gia.

Qun l Ri Ro

Qu trnh gim thiu s tip xc vi cc yu t khc c gi l qun l ri ro. Phng nga ri ro l mt trong nhng cch tt nht thc hin qun l ri ro trong thc t.

Phng v ri ro

Nu chng ta xc nh rng li nhun ca danh mc u t ph thuc vo th trng thng qua mi quan h ny

$$Y_{danh mc} = \alpha + \beta X_{VNINDEX}$$

th chng ta c th m mt v th bn khng trong VNINDEX c gng loi b ri ro ny. S lng chng ta m ra l $-\beta V$ trong $V$ l tng gi tr ca danh mc u t ca chng ta. iu ny c hiu qu v nu li nhun ca chng ta c xp x bi $\alpha + \beta X_{VNINDEX}$, th vic thm mt v th bn khng trong VNINDEX s lm cho li nhun mi ca chng ta tr thnh $\alpha + \beta X_{VNINDEX} - \beta X_{VNINDEX} = \alpha$. Li nhun ca chng ta gi y hon ton l alpha, iu ny khng ph thuc vo VNINDEX v s khng b nh hng bi ri ro t th trng.

Trung Lp vi Th Trng

Khi mt chin lc th hin beta n nh l 0, chng ta ni rng chin lc ny l trung lp vi th trng.

Vn Vi Vic c Tnh

Vn y l beta m chng ta c tnh khng nht thit s gi nguyn khi thi gian tri qua. Do , s lng v th bn m chng ta thc hin trong VNINDEX c th khng hon ton bo him cho danh mc u t ca chng ta, v trn thc t, vic gim beta mt cch ng k l kh kh khn.

Chng ta s ni nhiu hn v cc vn lin quan n vic c tnh cc tham s trong cc bi ging sau. Ngn gn, mi c tnh u c mt sai s chun tng ng vi n nh ca c tnh trong d liu quan st.

Thc hin bo him ri ro

By gi chng ta bit cn bo him bao nhiu, hy xem n nh hng n li nhun ca chng ta nh th no. Chng ta s xy dng danh mc u t ca mnh bng cch s dng ti sn v ch s tham chiu, cn nhc ch s tham chiu vi trng s $-\beta$ (m v chng ta ang bn khng n).

S nh i gia alpha v beta

Vic phng nga ri ro so vi mt ch s nh th trng thc s s gim li nhun ca bn khi th trng khng gp kh khn. Tuy nhin, iu ny hon ton n. Nu thut ton ca bn t bin ng hn, bn s c th s dng n by trong chin lc ca mnh v nhn li nhun v li mc ban u. Cn tt hn, li nhun ca bn s n nh hn nhiu so vi chin lc beta bin ng ban u.

Ni chung, mc d cc chin lc beta cao thng c v hp dn do li nhun cc k tt trong cc giai on tng trng ca th trng, nhng chng s tht bi trong di hn v s phi chu tn tht ln trong thi k suy thoi.

Cc loi phng v khc

Mc d chng ti s khng thc hin chng y, nhng c nhng chin lc phng v c th ph hp hn cho cc phng php u t khc.

Giao dch cp

Mt trong s l giao dch cp, trong mt ti sn th hai c s dng thay cho ch s chun y. iu ny s cho php bn, chng hn, loi b s bin ng trong mt ngnh bng cch mua c phiu ca mt cng ty v bn khng c phiu ca mt cng ty khc trong cng ngnh.

Danh mc c phiu Long-Short

Trong trng hp ny, chng ta xc nh mt bng xp hng trn mt nhm $n$ c phiu, sau mua di $p\%$ c phiu hng u v bn khng $p\%$ c phiu di cng vi lng tin bng nhau. iu ny c u im l c phng nga mt cch ngm, so vi r rng, khi $n$ ln. thy l do ti sao iu ny ng, hy tng tng vic mua mt b 100 chng khon ngu nhin. Xc sut rng h s beta tip xc th trng ca 100 chng khon ny xa 1.0 l rt thp, v chng ta ly mt mu ln t th trng. Tng t, khi chng ta xp hng theo mt ch s c lp no v mua 100 c phiu hng u, xc sut rng chng ta chn cc chng khon c beta tng th xa 1.0 l thp. Do , khi chn 100 c phiu Long v 100 c phiu Short, beta ca chin lc ny tr nn rt gn 1 - 1 = 0. R rng l mt s h thng xp hng s to ra thin lch mu v ph v gi nh ny, v d xp hng theo beta c tnh ca c phiu.

Mt li th khc ca cc chin lc c phiu Long-Short l bn ang t cc vo bng xp hng, hay ni cch khc l s khc bit trong hiu sut gia cc c phiu xp hng cao v thp. iu ny c ngha l bn khng cn phi lo lng v s nh i alpha/beta gp phi trong phng v ri ro.
