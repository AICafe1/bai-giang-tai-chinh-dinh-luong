Overfitting l g?

Khi xy dng mt m hnh, chng ta iu chnh c tham s v m hnh bng cch khp vi d liu mu. Sau , chng ta s dng m hnh v cc tham s d on d liu m chng ta cha quan st. Chng ta ni rng mt m hnh b overfit khi n qu nhy cm vi nhiu v cc c im ring l trong d liu mu, do khng phn nh ng qu trnh to d liu c bn.

hiu ti sao iu ny xy ra, chng ta phi xem xt lng nhiu c mt trong bt k tp d liu no. Chng ta c th coi mt tp d liu l $D_{T}$, d liu c bn thc s n t bt k qu trnh no m chng ta ang c gng m hnh ha, v $\epsilon$, mt s nhiu ngu nhin. Bi v nhng g chng ta thy l $D = D_{T} + \epsilon$, chng ta c th khp m hnh ca mnh d on rt chnh xc cho $\epsilon$, nhng khng phi cho $D_{T}$.

iu ny l vn bi v chng ta ch quan tm n vic khp vi mu trong chng mc m iu cung cp mt d on chnh xc cho d liu trong tng lai. Hai nguyn nhn chnh ca overfitting l:

  • kch thc mu nh, do nhiu v xu hng khng th phn bit c
  • chn mt m hnh qu phc tp, v vy m n cui cng b bp mo khp vi nhiu trong mu.

V d bng li: Qu nhiu quy tc ( phc tp)

Gi s bn c tp d liu sau:

Knh TV Cng nh sng trong phng S thch th
1 2 1
2 3 2
3 1 3

Bn ang c gng d on s thch th, v vy bn to ra cc quy tc sau:

  1. Nu Knh TV l 1 v Cng nh sng l 2, th S thch th s l 1.
  2. Nu Knh TV l 2 v Cng nh sng l 3, th S thch th s l 2.
  3. Nu Knh TV l 3 v Cng nh sng l 1, th S thch th s l 3.
  4. Trong tt c cc trng hp khc, d on mt s thch th trung bnh l 2.

y l mt m hnh c nh ngha r rng cho d liu tng lai, tuy nhin, trong trng hp ny, gi s s thch th ca bn hon ton ph thuc vo knh TV v khng ph thuc vo nh sng. Bi v chng ta c mt quy tc cho mi hng trong tp d liu ca chng ta, m hnh ca chng ta hon ton c th d on chnh xc trong d liu lch s ca chng ta, nhng s hot ng km trong cc th nghim thc t v chng ta ang qu khp vi ting n ngu nhin trong d liu cng nh sng.

Tng qut ha iu ny sang c phiu, nu m hnh ca bn bt u pht trin nhiu quy tc c th da trn cc s kin trong qu kh c th, th gn nh chc chn l bn ang qu khp. y l l do ti sao hc my hp en (mng n-ron, v.v.) li rt nguy him khi khng c thc hin ng cch.

V d: Khp ng cong

Hin tng overfitting d dng nhn thy nht khi chng ta nhn vo hi quy a thc. Di y, chng ta xy dng mt tp d liu theo dng bc hai cng vi nhiu. M hnh tuyn tnh b thiu st (underfit): cc m hnh tuyn tnh n gin khng ph hp cho tt c cc tnh hung, c bit khi chng ta c l do tin rng d liu l phi tuyn. ng cong bc hai c mt s li nhng khp vi d liu rt tt.

Khi chng ta khp mt a thc bc chn vi d liu, li l bng khng - mt a thc bc chn c th c xy dng i qua bt k 10 im no - nhng, nhn vo ui ca ng cong, chng ta bit rng khng th mong i n d on chnh xc cc mu khc t cng mt phn phi. N khp hon ho vi d liu, nhng l v n cng khp hon ho vi nhiu, v nhiu khng phi l iu chng ta mun m hnh ha. Trong trng hp ny, chng ta chn mt m hnh qu phc tp.

slr_prediction = slr.params.iloc[0] + slr.params.iloc[1]*x1

# Trade using a simple mean-reversion strategy
def trade(stock, length):

    # If window length is 0, algorithm doesn't make sense, so exit
    if length == 0:
        return 0

    # Compute rolling mean and rolling standard deviation
    rolling_window = stock.rolling(window=length)
    mu = rolling_window.mean()
    std = rolling_window.std()

    # Compute the z-scores for each day using the historical data up to that day
    zscores = (stock - mu)/std

    # Simulate trading
    # Start with no money and no positions
    money = 0
    count = 0
    for i in range(len(stock)):
        # Sell short if the z-score is > 1
        if zscores[i] > 1:
            money += stock[i]
            count -= 1
        # Buy long if the z-score is < 1
        elif zscores[i] < -1:
            money -= stock[i]
            count += 1
        # Clear positions if the z-score between -.5 and .5
        elif abs(zscores[i]) < 0.5:
            money += count*stock[i]
            count = 0
    return money

Kim Tra Cho

Kim tra cho l qu trnh chia d liu ca bn thnh $n$ phn, sau c lng cc tham s ti u cho $n-1$ phn kt hp v kim tra trn phn cui cng. Bng cch lm iu ny $n$ ln, mi ln cho mi phn c gi li, chng ta c th xc nh mc n nh ca cc c lng tham s ca chng ta v mc d on ca chng trn d liu khng n t tp d liu ban u.
