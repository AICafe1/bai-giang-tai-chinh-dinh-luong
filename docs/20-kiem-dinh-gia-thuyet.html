Kim nh gi thuyt thng k

by Maxwell Margenot and Delaney Granizo-Mackenzie. Reviewed by Gilbert Wasserman.

Edited by AI Cafe

Suy din thng k, thc hnh a ra d on v mt nhm ln da trn cc mu nh hn, thng c chia thnh hai phn, c lng v kim nh gi thuyt. c lng cung cp gi tr cho nhng iu c th m bn c th quan tm, chng hn nh trung bnh hoc phng sai, vi mt khong tin cy c cung cp. Mt khong tin cy cung cp mt vng m trong bn c th mong i tm thy gi tr thc ca tham s m bn c lng, v mt c lng gn nh khng bao gi chnh xc. Cc khong tin cy s dng mt mc tin cy c xc nh chn rng ca khong, t c tin cy cao hn, chng ta phi bo co mt khong rng hn. bit thm thng tin, vui lng xem bi ging v Khong Tin Cy.

V d, chng ta c th c lng mt gi tr trung bnh mu l $100$, vi mt khong tin cy t $90$ n $110$ mc tin cy $95\%$. iu ny khng c ngha l gi tr trung bnh ca qun th thc s nm gia $90$ v $110$ vi xc sut $95\%$, v gi tr trung bnh thc s l mt gi tr c nh v xc sut l $100\%$ hoc $0\%$, nhng chng ta khng bit gi tr no. Thay vo , ngha ca iu ny l qua nhiu ln tnh ton khong tin cy $95\%$ vi gi nh rng cc gi nh c bn v phn phi vn ng, gi tr trung bnh ca qun th s nm trong khong $95\%$ tng s ln.

iu ny cho chng ta mt tng v cc c im c th m mt qun th tng th c th th hin, da trn mt mu. Kim nh gi thuyt cung cp mt trng tm khc, chi tit mt khung cho vic kim tra thng k cc gi tr gi thuyt. Bng cch a ra mt khng nh v gi tr m mt gi tr nn c, bn to ra mt gi thuyt c th kim tra.

Mt iu cn nh l cc bi kim tra thng k c thit k sao cho nu tt c cc iu kin tin quyt u ng, bn nn nhn c cu tr li ng v d liu mt t l phn trm nht nh ca tng s ln. Khi bn chp nhn mt gi thuyt l ng da trn mt bi kim tra, iu khng c ngha l n chc chn ng. N ch c ngha l bn c th bit xc sut bn sai.

Gi thuyt Null v Gi thuyt Thay th

iu u tin chng ta cn gii thiu l gi thuyt null, thng c vit l $H_0$. Gi thuyt null l trng hp mc nh, thng phn nh nhn thc chung hin ti v th gii. Gi thuyt thay th l gi thuyt m bn ang kim tra.

V d

Gi thuyt thay th $H_A$ l bn s hu hn 10 i giy. Gi thuyt null $H_0$ l bn khng s hu hn 10 i giy.

Gi thuyt thay th $H_A$ l vic n pizza c lin quan n bo ph. Gi thuyt null $H_0$ l khng c mi lin quan.

Gi thuyt thay th $H_A$ l li nhun trung bnh ca FPT > 0. Gi thuyt null $H_0$ l li nhun trung bnh <= 0.

kh trong vic kim tra

Mt s gi thuyt d kim tra hn nhng gi thuyt khc. V d, gi thuyt thay th, "Ti s hu hn 10 i giy." v gi thuyt null i km rng bn khng s hu, d dng c kim tra bng cch m s i giy bn c. Tuy nhin, bn vn c th khng nhn c cu tr li hon ho mi lc trong trng hp ny, v c th c sai st trong vic o lng khi m, mc d kh nh.

Mt khc, gi thuyt "S lng cc loi cn trng ln hn s lng cc v sao trong v tr" s kh kim tra hn v yu cu thu thp nhiu d liu.

Gi thuyt phi c th kim tra

Mt gi thuyt khng th m h, nu khng th lm th no kim tra n? V d, "Giao dch theo xu hng l mt cch tt kim tin" thc s khng th kim tra. 'Tt' c ngha l g? Chng ta ang tho lun v loi giao dch theo xu hng no? Cc gi thuyt nn rt c th v loi kim tra cn thit nn theo ngay t gi thuyt.

Cch Thc Hin Kim nh Gi Thuyt

Cc bc chnh trong kim nh gi thuyt nh sau:

  1. a ra gi thuyt v gi thuyt thay th.
  2. Xc nh thng k kim nh ph hp v phn phi ca n. m bo rng tt c cc gi nh v d liu u c p ng (tnh dng, tnh chun, v.v.).
  3. Xc nh mc ngha, $\alpha$.
  4. T $\alpha$ v phn phi, tnh ton 'gi tr ti hn'.
  5. Thu thp d liu v tnh ton thng k kim nh.
  6. So snh thng k kim nh vi gi tr ti hn v quyt nh chp nhn hay bc b gi thuyt.

u tin, chng ta a ra gi thuyt m chng ta mun kim tra. Chng ta lm iu ny bng cch xc nh mt gi thuyt null v mt gi thuyt thay th. Gi thuyt null, $H_0$, l gi thuyt m chng ta mun kim tra, trong khi gi thuyt thay th, $H_A$, l gi thuyt c chp nhn trong trng hp $H_0$ b bc b.

Gi s rng chng ta mun kim tra xem li nhun trung bnh ca c phiu FPT c dng hay khng. Tham s m chng ta ang kim tra c k hiu l $\theta$ v gi tr xut ca tham s c k hiu l $\theta_0$, trong trng hp ny bng $0$. Vy chng ta ni rng $H_0$ ca chng ta l $\theta = \theta_0$, rng li nhun l m, v $H_A$ ca chng ta l $\theta \neq \theta_0$. Bao gm cu trc ny, c ba cch c th hnh thnh gi thuyt null v gi thuyt thay th:

  1. $H_0: \theta = \theta_0$ so vi $H_A: \theta \neq \theta_0$ (Gi thuyt thay th "khng bng")
  2. $H_0: \theta \leq \theta_0$ so vi $H_A: \theta > \theta_0$ (Gi thuyt thay th "ln hn")
  3. $H_0: \theta \geq \theta_0$ so vi $H_A: \theta < \theta_0$ (Gi thuyt thay th "nh hn")

Trong trng hp ny, khi chng ta ang kim tra li nhun ca FPT, $\theta = \mu_{FPT}$, i din cho li nhun trung bnh ca c phiu. V chng ta ang kim tra xem li nhun c dng hay m, chng ta c rng $\theta_0 = 0$. V d ca chng ta theo cu trc u tin ca mt bi kim tra gi thuyt. y l mt kim nh gi thuyt hai pha (hoc kim nh gi thuyt hai ui). Cu trc th hai v th ba l v d v kim nh gi thuyt mt pha (hoc kim nh gi thuyt mt ui). Vi mt kim nh mt pha, chng ta bc b gi thuyt null ng h gi thuyt thay th ch khi d liu cho thy rng $\theta$ ln hn hoc nh hn $\theta_0$. Mt kim nh hai pha bc b gi thuyt null ng h gi thuyt thay th nu d liu cho thy rng $\theta$ ln hn hoc nh hn $\theta_0$.

Vy nu chng ta vit gi thuyt ca mnh cho FPT bng nhng thut ng nh tnh hn, chng ta s c:

\begin{eqnarray} H_0 &:& \text{Li nhun trung bnh trn c phiu FPT l $0$}\\ H_A &:& \text{Li nhun trung bnh trn c phiu FPT khng phi l $0$} \end{eqnarray}

Khi hnh thnh mt bi kim nh gi thuyt, gi thuyt null v gi thuyt thay th phi b sung cho nhau. Gia chng phi bao trm tt c cc gi tr ca $\theta$. Bt k loi kim nh gi thuyt no m chng ta ang thc hin, chng ta lun kim tra gi thuyt null nh th $\theta = \theta_0$. Trong trng hp ca bt k kim nh mt pha no, iu ny vn s cung cp bng chng cho chng ta a ra quyt nh. V d, nu $H_0: \theta \leq 0$, $H_A: \theta > 0$, v chng ta c bng chng bc b $H_0: \theta = 0$ ng h $H_A: \theta > 0$, th iu ny cng ng cho tt c cc gi tr nh hn $0$.

Loi kim nh gi thuyt ph bin nht l kim nh hai pha, "khng bng", v n th hin mt quan im trung lp. Cc kim nh gi thuyt mt pha t trung lp hn so vi kim nh "khng bng", phn nh suy ngh ca ngi kim tra. Cc kim nh mt pha thng c s dng kim tra cc kt qu "mong mun" hoc cc kt qu m ngi kim tra c tng trc .

Ti sao tt c nhng iu ny l cn thit?

Ti sao chng ta khng ch cn ly trung bnh li nhun ca FPT v kim tra xem chng c > 0 khng? Bi v chng ta khng th nhn vo qu trnh to ra d liu thc t pha sau li nhun, chng ta ch c th ly mu li nhun trong mt khong thi gian hn ch no . Bi v chng ta ch quan st mt mu, mu c th phn nh ng hoc khng ng trng thi thc s ca qu trnh c bn. Chnh v s khng chc chn ny m chng ta cn s dng cc bi kim tra thng k.

Tip theo, chng ta xc nh thng k kim nh thch hp v phn phi xc sut ca n. Mt thng k kim nh thng c dng nh sau:

$$ \text{Thng k kim nh} = \frac{\text{Thng k mu} - \text{Gi tr ca tham s tng th di $H_0$ ($\theta_0$)}}{\text{Sai s chun ca thng k mu}} $$

Thng k kim nh c tnh ton da trn d liu mu v c so snh vi phn phi xc sut ca n xc nh xem c nn bc b gi thuyt khng hay khng bc b gi thuyt null. V chng ta ang kim tra li sut trung bnh trn c phiu FPT, chng ta c th s dng gi tr trung bnh mu, $\bar{X}_\mu$, lm thng k mu ca chng ta. Chng ta tnh sai s chun ca gi tr trung bnh mu l $\sigma_{\bar{X}} = \frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{n}}$ nu chng ta bit lch chun, $\sigma$, hoc l $s_{\bar{X}} = \frac{s}{\sqrt{n}}$, trong $s$ l lch chun mu. Vy nn, s dng nhng nh ngha ny, thng k kim nh ca chng ta c th c tnh nh sau:

$$ \frac{\bar{X}_\mu - \theta_0}{s_{\bar{X}}} = \frac{\bar{X}_\mu - 0}{s/\sqrt{n}} $$

Bn phn phi ph bin nht cho thng k kim nh nh sau:

  • Phn phi $t$ ($t$-test)
  • Phn phi chun ($z$-test)
  • Phn phi chi-bnh phng ($\chi^2$) ($\chi^2$-test)
  • Phn phi $F$ ($F$-test)

Chng ta s cp n chng mt cch chi tit sau. Hin ti, chng ta c th ni rng chng ta c th s dng mt bi kim tra $z$ vi cc gi nh ca chng ta trong v d FPT.

Sau khi xc nh thng k kim nh ph hp v phn phi xc sut, chng ta cn xc nh mc ngha ca bi kim tra, k hiu l $\alpha$. Cc gi tr m chng ta so snh thng k kim nh ca mnh bc b hoc khng bc b $H_0$ c xc nh da trn gi tr $\alpha$ ca chng ta.

Tnh hung Thc t
Quyt nh $H_0$ ng $H_0$ Sai
Khng bc b $H_0$ Quyt nh ng Li Loi II
Bc b $H_0$ (chp nhn $H_A$) Li Loi I Quyt nh ng

Mc ngha ca chng ta bng vi xc sut xy ra li loi I (mt "dng tnh gi"). Xc sut xy ra li loi II (mt "m tnh gi") c k hiu l $\beta$. Nu chng ta c gng gim xc sut xy ra li loi I, chng ta s tng xc sut xy ra li loi II, dn n mt s nh i. Cch duy nht gim xc sut ca c li loi I v li loi II xy ra l tng kch thc mu.

Cc mc ngha thng thng l $0.1$, $0.05$, v $0.01$. Bc b gi thuyt null ti $0.1$ c ngha l chng ta c mt s bng chng cho thy gi thuyt null l sai, $0.05$ c ngha l chng ta c bng chng mnh cho thy gi thuyt null l sai, bc b ti $0.01$ chng ta c bng chng rt mnh cho thy gi thuyt null l sai.

Gi tr ti hn (Critical Value)

By gi chng ta s xc nh gi tr ti hn, hay cn gi l im t chi. Gi tr ti hn cho thng k kim tra ca chng ta l gi tr m chng ta so snh vi thng k kim tra khi quyt nh xem c nn t chi gi thuyt null. Nu chng ta t chi gi thuyt null, chng ta ni rng kt qu l c ngha thng k, trong khi nu chng ta khng t chi gi thuyt null, chng ta ni rng kt qu l khng c ngha thng k.

Chng ta so snh thng k kim tra ca mnh vi mt gi tr ti hn quyt nh xem c t chi hay khng t chi gi thuyt null. Gi tr ti hn ca mt bi kim tra c xc nh da trn $\alpha$ ca kim tra gi thuyt ca chng ta cng nh phn phi chn. Trong trng hp ca chng ta, gi s $\alpha = 0.05$, v vy mc ngha ca chng ta l $0.05$. Vi kim tra $z$ mt pha, c hai cch khc nhau xem xt cc gi tr ti hn:

  • Nu chng ta kim tra $H_0$: $\theta \leq \theta_0$, $H_A$: $\theta > \theta_0$ ti $\alpha = 0.05$, gi tr ti hn ca chng ta l $z_{0.05} = 1.645$. V vy, chng ta so snh thng k kim tra ca mnh v chng ta t chi gi thuyt null nu $z > 1.645$.
  • Nu chng ta kim tra $H_0$: $\theta \geq \theta_0$, $H_A$: $\theta < \theta_0$ ti $\alpha = 0.05$, gi tr ti hn ca chng ta l $-z_{0.05} = -1.645$. Do , chng ta so snh thng k kim tra ca mnh v chng ta t chi gi thuyt khng nu $z < -1.645$.

Mt bi kim tra hai pha l mt tnh hung hi khc. V n l hai pha, c hai im t chi, m v dng. $\alpha$ ca chng ta l $0.05$, v vy tng xc sut ca li loi I phi cng li thnh $0.05$. Do , chng ta chia $0.05$ thnh hai phn cc im t chi ca chng ta l $z_{0.025}$ v $-z_{0.025}$ cho cc gi tr ti hn dng v m, tng ng. i vi mt bi kim tra $z$, cc gi tr ny l $1.96$ v $-1.96$. Do , chng ta t chi gi thuyt null nu $z < -1.96$ hoc nu $z > 1.96$. Nu chng ta thy rng $-1.96 \leq z \leq 1.96$, chng ta khng t chi gi thuyt null.

Khi tin hnh mt bi kim tra gi thuyt, bn cng c th s dng mt gi tr $p$ xc nh kt qu. Gi tr $p$ l mc ngha ti thiu m bn c th t chi gi thuyt null. Thng th mi ngi s din gii gi tr $p$ l "xc sut m gi thuyt null sai", nhng iu ny l gy hiu lm. Gi tr $p$ ch c ngha khi c so snh vi gi tr ngha. Nu gi tr $p$ nh hn $\alpha$, chng ta t chi gi thuyt null v ngc li. Gi tr $p$ thp hn khng lm cho mt th g "c ngha thng k hn". Rt nhiu u ra thng k s tnh ton gi tr $p$ cho bn, nhng cng c th tnh ton n mt cch th cng. Vic tnh ton ph thuc vo loi bi kim tra gi thuyt ca bn v CDF (c cp trong bi ging v Bin ngu nhin) ca phn phi m bn ang lm vic. tnh ton gi tr $p$ mt cch th cng, hy lm nh sau:

  • Trong mt bi kim tra gi thuyt 'nh hn hoc bng', gi tr $p$ l $1 - CDF(\text{Thng k kim tra})$
  • Trong mt bi kim tra gi thuyt 'ln hn hoc bng', gi tr $p$ l $CDF(\text{Thng k kim tra})$
  • Trong mt bi kim tra gi thuyt 'khng bng', gi tr $p$ l $2 * 1 - CDF(|\text{Thng k kim tra}|)$

Cc gi tr ngha kt ni rt tt vi cc khong tin cy, iu ny c cp su hn trong bi ging v Khong tin cy ca chng ta. Mt khong tin cy cung cp cho chng ta mt c lng v phm vi gi tr c th ca mt tham s da trn mt mc ngha nht nh. V d, nu khong tin cy $99\%$ ca chng ta cho trung bnh ca li nhun FPT l $(-0.0020, 0.0023)$, iu c ngha l c $99\%$ kh nng rng gi tr thc s ca trung bnh nm trong khong .

Kim nh gi thuyt v Trung bnh

Phn phi $z$, hay phn phi chun, l mt phn phi xc sut quan trng trong ti chnh. Chng ta thch khi mi th c phn phi theo quy lut chun v iu ny mang li nhiu tnh cht hu ch. Hn na, nhiu phng php c bn yu cu mt gi nh v tnh chun tin hnh. Tuy nhin, trong hu ht cc trng hp, phn phi $z$ s khng ph hp vi d liu ca chng ta. Chng ta him khi bit c gi tr tham s thc (trung bnh v phng sai) ca d liu v phi da vo cc xp x. Trong nhng trng hp ny, chng ta nn s dng phn phi $t$, mt xp x ca phn phi chun. Phn phi $t$ linh hot hn khi ni n kch thc mu nh v c thit k s dng vi trung bnh v phng sai mu. N c ui dy hn v nh thp hn, mang li nhiu s linh hot hn so vi phn phi chun.

C phn phi $t$ v $z$ u da vo mt gi nh c bn v tnh chun, iu ny thng ng khi chng ta phn tch d liu ti chnh. Do , ngoi vic kim tra cc trung bnh ring l, vic so snh gia hai hoc nhiu gi tr trung bnh l hp l. Chng ta c th s dng mt kim nh gi thuyt xc nh xem cc trung bnh ca mt s tp d liu c khc bit v mt thng k hay khng. y, chng ta s s dng phn phi $t$ minh ha. Chng ta s so snh li nhun trung bnh ca ch s VNINDEX v c phiu FPT bng mt kim nh gi thuyt xem liu s khc bit c c coi l c ngha thng k hay khng.

Trong khi nhng li sut ny c v c cng mt gi tr trung bnh, chng ta vn khng c bng chng khng nh iu . Chng ta s dng mt bi kim tra gi thuyt cng c nghi ng ca mnh trn c s thng k.

Khi so snh hai gi tr trung bnh, cc bi kim tra gi thuyt ca chng ta c nu nh sau:

  1. $H_0: \mu_1 - \mu_2 = \theta_0, \ H_A: \mu_1 - \mu_2 \neq \theta_0$
  2. $H_0: \mu_1 - \mu_2 \leq \theta_0, \ H_A: \mu_1 - \mu_2 > \theta_0$
  3. $H_0: \mu_1 - \mu_2 \geq \theta_0, \ H_A: \mu_1 - \mu_2 < \theta_0$

Trong $\mu_1, \mu_2$ l cc gi tr trung bnh tng ng ca VNINDEX v FPT v $\theta_0$ l tham s m chng ta ang kim tra. Chng ta s s dng bi kim tra gi thuyt u tin kim tra s bnh ng ca hai li sut. Nu chng ta gi nh rng phng sai ca tng th l nh nhau, th thng k kim tra ca chng ta c tnh nh sau:

$$ t = \frac{(\bar{X}_1 - \bar{X}_2) - (\mu_1 - \mu_2)}{(\frac{s_p^2}{n_1} + \frac{s_p^2}{n_2})^{1/2}} = \frac{\bar{X}_1 - \bar{X}_2}{(\frac{s_p^2}{n_1} + \frac{s_p^2}{n_2})^{1/2}} $$

Vi $s_p^2 = \frac{(n_1 - 1)s_1^2 + (n_2 - 1)s_2^2)}{n_1 + n_2 - 2}$ l c lng ca phng sai chung, c gi l phng sai gp, v $n_1 + n_2 - 2$ l s bc t do ($n_1 - 1$ v $n_2 - 1$ cho mi tp d liu). Mt bi kim tra $t$-test thng thng v mt gi tr trung bnh gi nh rng tt c cc phng sai lin quan l nh nhau vi cc phn phi chun c bn. Nu chng ta gi nh rng cc phng sai khng bng nhau, chng ta phi tnh ton thng k kim tra ca mnh theo cch khc. Thng k kim tra trong trng hp ny l:

$$ t = \frac{(\bar{X}_1 - \bar{X}_2) - (\mu_1 - \mu_2)}{(\frac{s_1^2}{n_1} + \frac{s_2^2}{n_2})^{1/2}} = \frac{\bar{X}_1 - \bar{X}_2}{(\frac{s_1^2}{n_1} + \frac{s_2^2}{n_2})^{1/2}} $$

Trong s bc t do c s dng tm thng k quan trng l bc t do c sa i, tc l s gi tr c th thay i, $df = \frac{(\frac{s_1^2}{n_1} + \frac{s_2^2}{n_2})^2}{\frac{(s_1^2/n_1)^2}{n_1} + \frac{(s_2^2/n_2)^2}{n_2}}$. iu ny bo tn tnh chun c bn ca d liu ang c kim tra trong khi tnh n s khc bit v phng sai. Tnh ton thng k theo cch ny loi b nhiu vn c th xy ra nu chng ta c cc phng sai khng bng nhau, c bit l nu kch thc mu ca d liu c bn cng khc nhau. Trng hp c th ny ca bi kim tra $t$ c gi l "Bi kim tra $t$ ca Welch vi phng sai khng bng nhau".

Trong v d ny, chng ta gi nh rng phng sai ca li sut VNINDEX v FPT l khc nhau. Chng ta ngh rng FPT s c ri ro cao hn VNINDEX nn chng ta s s dng cng thc th hai ca thng k kim tra. Gi s rng $\alpha = 0.05$ v vy chng ta ang tnh ton mt bi kim tra gi thuyt 95%.

# Sample mean values

Khi xem xt bng phn phi t, chng ta xc nh rng cc gi tr quan trng cho bi kim tra gi thuyt hai pha ca chng ta l $-1.96$ v $1.96$. Thng k kim tra ca chng ta nm gia cc gi tr ny, v vy chng ta khng bc b gi thuyt null v xc nh rng s khc bit gia li nhun ca VNINDEX v FPT l khng khc bit ng k so vi $0$.

Kim nh Gi thuyt v Phn Tn

Nu chng ta mun kim tra phn tn ca cc qun th, chng ta cn s dng mt phn phi khc vi phn phi $t$ v $z$. phn tn phi theo nh ngha ln hn (hoc bng) $0$ v thc t l cc phn phi m chng ta lm vic cho n nay cho php cc gi tr m khin chng khng kh thi nh cc phn phi kim tra. Ri ro c nh lng theo lch chun v phn tn, v vy phng php kim nh gi thuyt ny l mt b sung hu ch cho b cng c ti chnh ca chng ta.

Thay v cc phn phi $t$ v $z$, chng ta s lm vic vi cc phn phi $\chi^2$ cho cc bi kim tra phn tn n v cc phn phi $F$ cho vic so snh phn tn. Nhng phn phi ny c gii hn di l $0$, lm cho chng kh thi cho vic kim tra theo cch ny.

Cng ging nh vi tt c cc bi kim tra gi thuyt khc ca chng ta, cc bi kim tra phn tn n c th c ba dng:

  1. $H_0: \sigma^2 = \sigma_0^2, \ H_A: \sigma^2 \neq \sigma_0^2$
  2. $H_0: \sigma^2 \leq \sigma_0^2, \ H_A: \sigma^2 > \sigma_0^2$
  3. $H_0: \sigma^2 \geq \sigma_0^2, \ H_A: \sigma^2 < \sigma_0^2$

Phn phi $\chi^2$ l mt h cc hm vi mi cng thc khc nhau c xc nh bi s bc t do. Hnh dng ca phn phi khc nhau cho mi gi tr khc nhau ca s bc t do, $k$.

Chng ta tnh ton thng k kim nh $\chi^2$ nh sau:

$$ \chi^2 = \frac{(n - 1)s^2}{\sigma_0^2} $$

Trong , $s^2$ l phng sai mu v $n$ l kch thc ca tp d liu. S bc t do l $n - 1$ v iu ny c s dng cng vi thng k kim nh xc nh gi tr ngng ca kim nh gi thuyt $\chi^2$ ca chng ta.

$$ H_0: \sigma^2 \leq 0.0001, \ H_A: \sigma^2 > 0.0001 $$

So snh Hai Phng Sai

Chng ta c th so snh phng sai ca hai bin khc nhau bng cch s dng phn phi $F$. Khi xy dng mt so snh phng sai bng cch s dng kim nh $F$, cc gi thuyt c thit lp nh sau:

  1. $H_0: \sigma_1^2 = \sigma_2^2, \ H_A: \sigma_1^2 \neq \sigma_2^2$
  2. $H_0: \sigma_1^2 \leq \sigma_2^2, \ H_A: \sigma_1^2 > \sigma_2^2$
  3. $H_0: \sigma_1^2 \geq \sigma_2^2, \ H_A: \sigma_1^2 < \sigma_2^2$

Phn phi $F$ tng t nh phn phi $\chi^2$ ch n khng i xng v b gii hn di bi $0$. Phn phi $F$ c xc nh vi hai gi tr bc t do khc nhau. i vi mc ch kim nh gi thuyt, mi bc t do tng ng vi mt trong cc yu t m chng ta ang so snh. Mt bin ngu nhin $F$ c th c xy dng t hai phn phi $\chi^2$ khc nhau. $X$ l mt bin ngu nhin $F$ nu n c th c vit di dng $X = \frac{Y_1/d_1}{Y_2/d_2}$, trong $Y_1$ v $Y_2$ l cc bin ngu nhin $\chi^2$ vi bc t do ln lt l $d_1$ v $d_2$.

Bin ngu nhin $F$ thc cht l t l ca cc phng sai. Do , vic xy dng thng k kim nh $F$ c thc hin bng cch ly t l ca cc phng sai mu ca d liu m chng ta mun kim tra. Chng ta c th n gin chn $\sigma_1^2$ v $\sigma_2^2$ i din cho mt trong cc phng sai m chng ta ang so snh, cho thng k $F$ ca chng ta ln hn $1$.

$$ F = \frac{s_1^2}{s_2^2} $$

Hy so snh VNINDEX v FPT xem liu phng sai ca chng c ging nhau khng (mt kim nh gi thuyt 'khng bng'). Chng ta s s dng mc ngha $\alpha = 0.05$. Nh rng i vi mt kim nh hai pha, chng ta tnh ton cc gi tr ti hn di v trn bng cch s dng cc gi tr ca $\alpha/2$. Chng ta thu thp d liu v tnh ton thng k kim nh.
