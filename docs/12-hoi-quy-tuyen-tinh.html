Hi quy tuyn tnh

By Evgenia "Jenny" Nitishinskaya and Delaney Granizo-Mackenzie with example algorithms by David Edwards

Edited by AI Cafe

Hi quy tuyn tnh l mt k thut o lng mi quan h gia hai bin. Nu chng ta c mt bin c lp $X$ v mt bin ph thuc $Y$, hi quy tuyn tnh cho php chng ta xc nh m hnh tuyn tnh no $Y = \alpha + \beta X$ gii thch tt nht d liu. V d, hy xem xt FPT v VCB. Chng ta mun bit VCB bin i nh th no da trn s bin i ca FPT, v vy chng ta s ly li nhun hng ngy ca mi cng ty v hi quy chng vi nhau.

Th vin statsmodels ca Python c mt hm ph hp tuyn tnh tch hp sn. Lu rng iu ny s cho ra mt ng ph hp tt nht; liu mi quan h m n th hin c ngha hay khng l iu bn cn xc nh. u ra cng s c mt s thng k v m hnh, chng hn nh R-bnh phng v gi tr F, iu ny c th gip bn nh lng tt ca s ph hp thc s l nh th no.

# Import libraries
import numpy as np

u tin, chng ta s nh ngha mt hm thc hin hi quy tuyn tnh v v biu kt qu.

In[2]:
def linreg(X,Y):
    # Running the linear regression
    X = sm.add_constant(X)
    model = regression.linear_model.OLS(Y, X).fit()
    a = model.params[0]
    b = model.params[1]
    X = X[:, 1]

    # Return summary of the regression and plot results
    X2 = np.linspace(X.min(), X.max(), 100)
    Y_hat = X2 * b + a
    plt.scatter(X, Y, alpha=0.3) # Plot the raw data
    plt.plot(X2, Y_hat, 'r', alpha=0.9);  # Add the regression line, colored in red
    plt.xlabel('X Value')
    plt.ylabel('Y Value')
    return model.summary()

Hi quy tuyn tnh vs. Tng quan

  • Hi quy tuyn tnh cung cp cho chng ta mt m hnh tuyn tnh c th, nhng b gii hn trong cc trng hp ph thuc tuyn tnh.
  • Tng quan th chung hn cho c ph thuc tuyn tnh v khng tuyn tnh, nhng khng cung cp cho chng ta mt m hnh thc t.
  • C hai u l nhng thc o ca hip phng sai.
  • Hi quy tuyn tnh c th cho chng ta mi quan h gia Y v nhiu bin c lp bng cch lm cho X tr thnh a chiu.

Tham S khc vi c Lng

iu quan trng l phi nh rng tt c cc tham s $\alpha$ v $\beta$ c c lng bi hi quy tuyn tnh ch l c lng. Bn khng bao gi c th bit cc tham s tht s l g tr khi bn bit qu trnh vt l to ra d liu. Cc tham s m bn c lng hm nay c th khng ging nh phn tch c thc hin khi bao gm d liu ngy mai, v cc tham s tht s c th ang thay i. Do , iu quan trng l khi thc hin phn tch thc t phi ch n sai s chun ca cc c lng tham s. Mt cch c cm nhn v n nh ca cc c lng tham s ca bn l c lng chng bng cch s dng mt ca s d liu ng v xem c bao nhiu bin ng trong cc c lng.

Trong trng hp ny, ng hi quy tt nht thc s m hnh ha bin ph thuc Y kh tt (vi gi tr $R^2$ cao).

nh gi v bo co kt qu

M hnh hi quy da trn mt s gi nh:

  • Bin c lp khng phi l ngu nhin.
  • Phng sai ca sai s l khng i gia cc quan st. iu ny rt quan trng nh gi ph hp ca m hnh.
  • Cc sai s khng t tng quan. Thng k Durbin-Watson pht hin iu ny; nu n gn bng 2, th khng c t tng quan.
  • Cc sai s c phn phi theo phn phi chun. Nu iu ny khng ng, chng ta khng th s dng mt s thng k, chng hn nh kim nh F.

Nu chng ta xc nhn rng cc gi nh cn thit ca m hnh hi quy c tha mn, chng ta c th an tm s dng cc thng k c bo co phn tch ph hp. V d, gi tr $R^2$ cho bit phn trm bin ng tng th ca $Y$ c m hnh gii thch.

Khi a ra d on da trn m hnh, iu hu ch l khng ch bo co mt gi tr duy nht m cn l khong tin cy. Hi quy tuyn tnh bo co khong tin cy 95% cho cc tham s hi quy, v chng ta c th hnh dung iu ny bng cch s dng th vin seaborn, th vin ny v ng hi quy v lm ni bt khong tin cy 95% (mc nh) cho ng hi quy:

Phng php bnh phng ti thiu

Hi quy hot ng bng cch ti u ha v tr ca ng hi quy tt nht (hoc mt phng trong cc chiu cao hn). N thc hin iu ny bng cch xc nh mc sai lch ca hi quy thng qua mt hm mc tiu. Trong hi quy bnh phng ti thiu (OLS), m chng ta ang s dng y, hm mc tiu l:

$$\sum_{i=1}^n (Y_i - a - bX_i)^2$$

Chng ta s dng $a$ v $b$ i din cho cc ng vin tim nng cho $\alpha$ v $\beta$. ngha ca hm mc tiu ny l cho mi im trn ng hi quy tt nht, chng ta so snh n vi im thc t v ly bnh phng ca s chnh lch. Hm ny s gim khi chng ta c c cc c lng tham s tt hn. Hi quy l mt trng hp n gin ca ti u ha s hc c gii php dng ng v khng cn bt k b ti u ha no. Chng ta ch cn tm cc kt qu nhm ti thiu ha hm mc tiu.

Chng ta s k hiu m hnh cui cng m kt qu t vic ti thiu ha hm mc tiu ca chng ta l:

$$ \hat{Y} = \hat{\alpha} + \hat{\beta}X $$

Vi $\hat{\alpha}$ v $\hat{\beta}$ l cc c lng chn cho cc tham s m chng ta s dng d on v $\hat{Y}$ l cc gi tr d on ca $Y$ da trn cc c lng .

Sai S Chun

Chng ta cng c th tnh sai s chun ca c lng, o lng lch chun ca thnh phn sai s $\epsilon$, bng cch ly tham s scale ca m hnh c tr v bi hi quy v ly cn bc hai ca n. Cng thc cho sai s chun ca c lng l

$$ s = \left( \frac{\sum_{i=1}^n \epsilon_i^2}{n-2} \right)^{1/2} $$

Nu $\hat{\alpha}$ v $\hat{\beta}$ l cc tham s thc s ($\hat{\alpha} = \alpha$ v $\hat{\beta} = \beta$), chng ta c th biu din sai s cho mt gi tr d on c th ca $Y$ l $s^2$ cho tt c cc gi tr ca $X_i$. Chng ta tnh bnh phng s chnh lch $(Y - \hat{Y})$ c c phng sai v $\hat{Y}$ khng bao gm sai s trong chnh cc c lng tham s. Bi v $\hat{\alpha}$ v $\hat{\beta}$ ch l cc c lng trong vic xy dng m hnh ca $Y$, bt k gi tr d on no, $\hat{Y}$, s c sai s chun ring ca n da trn phn phi ca cc bin $X$ m chng ta a vo m hnh. Sai s d on ny c biu din bng cng thc sau:

$$ s_f^2 = s^2 \left( 1 + \frac{1}{n} + \frac{(X - \mu_X)^2}{(n-1)\sigma_X^2} \right) $$

Trong $\mu_X$ l trung bnh ca cc quan st ca chng ta v $X$ v $\sigma_X$ l lch chun ca $X$. iu chnh ny cho $s^2$ bao gm s khng chc chn trong cc c lng tham s ca chng ta. Sau , khong t tin 95% cho d on l $\hat{Y} \pm t_cs_f$, trong $t_c$ l gi tr ti hn ca thng k t cho $n$ mu v tin cy mong mun l 95%.
