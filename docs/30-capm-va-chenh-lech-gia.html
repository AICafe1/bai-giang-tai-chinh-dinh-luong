M hnh nh gi ti sn (CAPM) v l thuyt chnh lch gi

by Beha Abasi, Maxwell Margenot, and Delaney Granizo-Mackenzie

M hnh nh gi Ti sn Vn (CAPM) l mt thc o kinh in v chi ph vn. N thng c s dng trong ti chnh nh gi gi tr ca ti sn v nh gi tc ng ca phn b ri ro t th trng ni chung. Trong bi ging ny, chng ta s tho lun v CAPM v L thuyt nh gi Dch chuyn (APT) tng qut hn hnh thnh c s cho vic nh gi ri ro lin quan n cc yu t khc nhau.

Ri ro c th v Ri ro H thng

Ni chung, danh mc u t v ti sn c th phi i mt vi hai loi ri ro: ri ro c th v ri ro h thng. Ri ro c th cp n nhng ri ro c th cho tng cng ty v c th c a dng ha, chng hn nh thay i trong ban qun l hoc sn xut b li, trong khi ri ro h thng l ri ro trn ton th trng v nh hng n tt c cc thnh vin trong th trng. Mt v d c th l s chm li ca nn kinh t hoc thay i trong li sut. V tt c cc cng ty u chu nh hng ca ri ro h thng, nn n khng th c a dng ha.

Phn thng Ri ro

Khi s lng ti sn trong mt danh mc u t tng ln, nhiu ri ro c th s b loi b v c a dng ha. y l l do chnh khin chng ta mun trnh ri ro tp trung vo mt v th. Khi danh mc u t ca bn ln hn v thc hin nhiu ln cc c lp thng qua vic a dng ha, phng sai ca danh mc u t s gim cho n khi ch cn li ri ro h thng. V chng ta khng th loi b ri ro h thng, cc nh u t phi c nhn mt phn thng ri ro cao hn mc li sut khng ri ro b p cho ri ro m h phi gnh chu khi u t vo danh mc ny. Nhng ri ro cp cng ty ring l trong danh mc ny khng c phn thng lin quan v iu ny s to ra c hi chnh lch gi. Cc c ng c th thu thp phn thng ri ro trong khi a dng ha ri ro lin quan n h. iu ny c ngha l li nhun b sung m khng c thm mc ri ro no. y l nh ngha ca mt c hi chnh lch gi!

T l lun ny, chng ta c th kt lun rng mc phn thng ri ro ca mt ti sn khng nn c mi quan h vi ri ro c th ca n, m thay vo ch nn da vo mc ri ro h thng m n mang li. tnh ton chnh xc mc phn thng ri ro ca mt ti sn, v do l li nhun k vng ca chng ta, chng ta cn tm mt thc o v ri ro h thng. Nu chng ta c iu , th chng ta c th nh ngha l thuyt li nhun ca mt ti sn theo cch sau:

$$E[\mbox{Li nhun}] = \mbox{Li sut khng ri ro} + \mbox{Phn thng ri ro}$$

Mt cch lm iu ny l c lng cch m s thay i trong li sut vt tri ca mt ti sn lin quan n s thay i trong li sut vt tri ca th trng. Biu din iu ny di dng hi quy tuyn tnh s cho chng ta mi quan h nh l s thay i trong li nhun k vng ca mt ti sn cho mi 1% thay i trong li sut ca danh mc th trng.

V l thuyt, danh mc th trng ny khng nn cn ri ro c th phn tn v do ch dao ng vi cc c sc h thng. Trong thc t, chng ta s dng mt ch s th trng nh VNINDEX lm i din cho danh mc th trng. Beta m chng ta nhn c t vic hi quy li sut ca mt ti sn vi li sut ca th trng s l thc o ri ro h thng ca chng ta. Beta ny i din cho nhy ca dng li sut ca mt ti sn i vi cc c sc ton th trng.

Vi beta ny, phn thng ri ro ca ti sn $i$ c nh ngha l:

$$\mbox{Phn thng ri ro ca Ti sn}_i = \beta (\mbox{Phn thng ri ro th trng})$$

Chng ta gi m hnh n gin ny l M hnh nh gi Ti sn Vn (CAPM).

L thuyt nh gi Ti sn Vn

Chng ta c th din t CAPM mt cch r rng hn nh sau:

$$E[R_i] = R_F + \beta(E[R_M] - R_F)$$

Trong $R_i$ l li sut ca ti sn $i$, $R_F$ l t l khng ri ro, v $R_M$ l li sut ca th trng. CAPM l mt trong nhng thc o c bn nht v chi ph vn. N xc nh li sut ti thiu cn thit thu ht cc nh u t nm gi mt ti sn nht nh.

Ni mt cch khc, CAPM cho rng li sut ca mt ti sn nn l t l khng ri ro, m l iu chng ta yu cu b p cho lm pht v gi tr thi gian ca tin, cng nh mt khon b sung b p cho mc ri ro h thng m chng ta ang phi i mt.

np.random.seed(123)

# Turn off progress printing

# Number of assets
n_assets = 4

# Number of observations
n_obs = 2000

## Generating random returns for our 4 securities
return_vec = np.random.randn(n_assets, n_obs)

def rand_weights(n):
    '''
    Produces n random weights that sum to 1
    '''
    k = np.random.rand(n)
    return k / sum(k)

def random_portfolio(returns):
    '''
    Returns the mean and standard deviation of returns for a random portfolio
    '''

    p = np.asmatrix(np.mean(returns, axis=1))
    w = np.asmatrix(rand_weights(returns.shape[0]))
    C = np.asmatrix(np.cov(returns))

    mu = w * p.T
    sigma = np.sqrt(w * C * w.T)

    # This recursion reduces outliers to keep plots pretty
    if sigma > 2:
        return random_portfolio(returns)
    return mu, sigma

def optimal_portfolios(returns):
    n = len(returns)
    returns = np.asmatrix(returns)

    N = 100000

    # Creating a list of returns to optimize the risk for
    mus = [100**(5.0 * t/N - 1.0) for t in range(N)]

    # Convert to cvxopt matrices
    S = opt.matrix(np.cov(returns))
    pbar = opt.matrix(np.mean(returns, axis=1))

    # Create constraint matrices
    G = -opt.matrix(np.eye(n))   # negative n x n identity matrix
    h = opt.matrix(0.0, (n ,1))
    A = opt.matrix(1.0, (1, n))
    b = opt.matrix(1.0)

    # Calculate efficient frontier weights using quadratic programming
    portfolios = [solvers.qp(mu*S, -pbar, G, h, A, b)['x']
                  for mu in mus]

    ## Calculate the risk and returns of the frontier
    returns = [blas.dot(pbar, x) for x in portfolios]
    risks = [np.sqrt(blas.dot(x, S*x)) for x in portfolios]

    return returns, risks

def maximize_sharpe_ratio(return_vec, risk_free_rate):
    """
    Finds the CAPM optimal portfolio from the efficient frontier
    by optimizing the Sharpe ratio.
    """

    def find_sharpe(weights):

        means = [np.mean(asset) for asset in return_vec]

        numerator = sum(weights[m]*means[m] for m in range(len(means))) - risk_free_rate

        denominator = np.sqrt(weights.T.dot(np.corrcoef(return_vec).dot(weights)))

        return numerator/denominator

    guess = np.ones(len(return_vec)) / len(return_vec)

    def objective(weights):
        return -find_sharpe(weights)

    # Set up equality constrained
    cons = {'type':'eq', 'fun': lambda x: np.sum(np.abs(x)) - 1}

    # Set up bounds for individual weights
    bnds = [(0, 1)] * len(return_vec)

    results = optimize.minimize(objective, guess,
                            constraints=cons, bounds=bnds,
                            method='SLSQP', options={'disp': False})

    return results

risk_free_rate = np.mean(R_F)

results = maximize_sharpe_ratio(return_vec, risk_free_rate)

L thuyt nh gi chnh lch

M hnh CAPM, mc d c s dng v nghin cu rng ri, c nhiu nhc im. Vi nhng gi nh nghim ngt v hn ch, n khng hot ng tt trong cc th nghim thc nghim. L thuyt nh gi chnh lch (APT) nhm mc ch tng qut ha m hnh CAPM, v cc ti sn c th b nh hng bi cc loi ri ro khc ngoi ri ro th trng v cc nh u t c th quan tm n nhng yu t khc ngoi trung bnh v phng sai.

APT l mt l thuyt nh gi ti sn da vo vic din t li nhun bng mt m hnh yu t tuyn tnh:

$$R_i = a_i + b_{i1} F_1 + b_{i2} F_2 + \ldots + b_{iK} F_K + \epsilon_i$$

Mt yu t l mt chui li nhun c xc nh hon ton bi mt s c im no . V d, CAPM ch c mt yu t, l li nhun th trng. Nu chng ta m hnh ha t l li nhun ca mnh nh trn, th li nhun k vng s c dng:

$$ E(R_i) = R_F + b_{i1} \lambda_1 + b_{i2} \lambda_2 + \ldots + b_{iK} \lambda_K $$

trong $R_F$ l li sut phi ri ro, v $\lambda_j$ l phn thng ri ro - li nhun vt qu li sut phi ri ro - cho yu t $j$. Phn thng ny pht sinh v cc nh u t yu cu li nhun cao hn b p cho vic chu ri ro cao hn.

Chng ta s tnh ton phn thng ri ro cho cc yu t ca mnh bng hi quy Fama-Macbeth. Tuy nhin, c nhiu cch khc nhau tnh ton mi $\lambda_j$!

Kinh doanh chnh lch gi

By gi chng ta c mt phng php kh tng qut tnh ton li nhun k vng, chng ta c th tho lun v kinh doanh chnh lch gi mt cch k thut hn. Thng thng c rt nhiu chng khon trong tp v tr ca chng ta. Nu chng ta s dng cc chng khon khc nhau tnh ton $\{\lambda_i\}$, liu kt qu ca chng ta c nht qun khng? Nu kt qu ca chng ta khng nht qun, s c mt c hi kinh doanh chnh lch gi (theo k vng), mt hot ng to ra li nhun m khng phi chu ri ro v khng cn u t tin net. Trong trng hp ny, chng ta c ngha rng c mt hot ng khng ri ro vi li nhun k vng dng m khng yu cu u t net. iu ny xy ra khi cc k vng v li nhun khng nht qun, tc l ri ro khng c nh gi mt cch nht qun gia cc chng khon.

Gi s rng c mt ti sn vi t l li nhun k vng l 0.2 cho nm ti v mt $\beta$ l 1.2 so vi th trng, trong khi th trng k vng c t l li nhun l 0.1, v t l khng ri ro trn tri phiu 1 nm l 0.05. Khi , m hnh APT cho chng ta bit rng t l li nhun k vng trn ti sn nn l

$$ R_F + \beta \lambda = 0.05 + 1.2 (0.1 - 0.05) = 0.11$$

iu ny khng ng ngha vi d on rng ti sn s c t l li nhun l 0.2. Vy, nu chng ta mua 100 ng ca ti sn ca mnh, bn khng 120 ng ca th trng, v mua 20 ng tri phiu, chng ta s khng u t tin net v khng phi chu bt k ri ro h thng no (chng ta trung lp vi th trng), nhng chng ta k vng s kim c $0.2(100) - 0.1(120) + 0.05(20) = 9$ ng vo cui nm.

M hnh APT gi nh rng nhng c hi ny s c tn dng cho n khi gi c thay i v cc c hi kinh doanh chnh lch gi bin mt. Ngha l, n gi nh rng c nhng nh kinh doanh chnh lch gi c kin nhn v vn. iu ny cung cp mt l do cho vic s dng cc m hnh yu t thc nghim trong nh gi chng khon: nu m hnh khng nht qun, s c mt c hi kinh doanh chnh lch gi, v do cc gi c s iu chnh.

t cc C Hai Chiu

Vic bit chnh xc $E(R_i)$ l rt kh khn, nhng m hnh ny cho chng ta bit li sut k vng nn l g nu th trng khng c c hi chnh lch gi. iu ny to nn tng cho cc chin lc da trn h thng xp hng m hnh yu t. Nu bn c mt m hnh cho li sut k vng ca mt ti sn, th bn c th xp hng cc ti sn da trn hiu sut k vng ca chng v s dng thng tin ny thc hin giao dch. Vic to ra mt h thng xp hng l du n ca mt chin lc c phiu Long-Short.
